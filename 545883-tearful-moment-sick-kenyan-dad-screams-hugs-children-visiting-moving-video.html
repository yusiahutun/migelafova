    Tearful Moment as Sick Kenyan Dad Screams, Hugs Children after Visiting Him in Moving Video

    Every parent hopes to raise responsible children who will care for them in their time of weakness. Caring children have reduced their unwell father to tears after paying him a surprise visit at home. The sick father could not control his overwhelming emotions when his children entered the house.

    16 Jul 2024, 00:00
    • Michael Obonyo was seated peacefully in the house when his children stormed in, and he could not hold back his emotions
    • The man of God who had been unwell ran to the wall and began to weep as his children came to comfort him
    • Each one of them gave him a tight hug as social media users expressed mixed reactions in the comment section

    "So emotional as these children paid a surprise visit to their dad, Obonyo Michael, who has been unwell for some time. Tears of joy. May your dad receive healing in Jesus' name," read the video's caption by Facebook user Derrick Mbugua.

    Obonyo sat comfortably on a lovely couch as he relaxed in the house, unaware of the guests.

    When they entered, he let out a shout and shot off from the couch, running to the wall, surprised to see his children.

    Redeemed Gospel Church Huruma Kenya pastor

    He then bent to a chair that was next to the wall as he sobbed while one young man approached to console him.

    As he sat on the chair, the children came to him individually, giving him a heartfelt embrace.

    When a young lady came, they hugged for a long time, and Obonyo, a pastor at Redeemed Gospel Church Huruma Kenya, shed tears in her arms.
