This Thai dish of charcoal-grilled steak, served thinly sliced with a sauce containing copious amounts of chilli, seems straightforward enough. One story the dish's name is said to originate from involves a tiger that once killed a cow. He found the loin and began his meal there, enjoying the bovine's most tender sections, right up to the neck.
