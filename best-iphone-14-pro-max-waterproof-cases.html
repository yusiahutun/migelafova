    FAQ

    Q: Is the iPhone 14 Pro Max Waterproof?

    According to Apple, the iPhone 14 Pro Max and 14 Pro are rated IP68 for water resistance, allowing them to withstand splashes and brief submersion in water up to a depth of 20 feet for 30 minutes. While the devices are water-resistant, they may still require a waterproof case for enhanced protection against water damage.

    Q: Can You Take Underwater Photos With iPhone 14 Pro Max?

    You can safely take underwater photos with the iPhone 14 Pro Max using a waterproof case or phone pouch. Most waterproof cases are watertight, allowing you to take underwater pictures and engage in outdoor activities while keeping your iPhone safe and dry.

    Q: Does iPhone 14 Need a Waterproof Case?

    You'll need a waterproof case for your iPhone 14 if you engage in water sports and outdoor activities like swimming and fishing. Other than that, most standard cases and the iPhone's water-resistant features can stand up to minor splashes and exposure to liquids.
