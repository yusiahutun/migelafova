    I’ve always noticed this small bald patch near my hairline that’s particularly visible when I tie up my hair. Turns out, it’s actually a bunch of baby hairs that never fully grew. I’d accepted it and decided to embrace the patchiness — until Style Edit’s Instant Root Cover-Up Stick entered the chat.

    The dual-end marker offers a sweat- and rain-resistant solution for sparse areas that comes in four brown and black shades. The waxless formula works on all hair types and comes in a compact 3-inch pocket size for on-the-go touch-ups. Simply apply the cream-to-powder formula to the desired area, then stipple using the sponge tip to achieve a natural look. It’s easy, fast, and effective. 

    Style Edit Instant Root Cover-Up Stick in Black

    I rely on the Style Edit Instant Root Cover-Up Stick almost daily, filling in bald spots by running the product along my hairline and middle part. It creates the illusion of volume, making my dark brown hair stand out. Even better, I’ve worn this hair concealer for upwards of 12 hours at a time. It’s never rubbed off, flaked, or left my head itchy or dry. Removing the product is just as easy as applying; I simply wash it out with shampoo and voilà!         

    The Style Edit Cover-Up Stick isn’t just for hiding bald spots; it’s also genius for concealing gray hairs. In fact, this $36 product is Marisa Tomei’s secret weapon for disguising silver strands. “I have a little thing for my grays, which is fine for the ripe old age of 57,” Tomei said in a 2021 Vogue Beauty Secrets video. The color payoff was immediately noticeable as she applied it, creating the illusion of thicker, more even-toned hair.

    Style Edit Instant Root Cover-Up Stick in Light Brown

    Even if you’re not trying to tone-down white hair, the cream cover-up is brilliant for hiding root regrowth after dying your mane a lighter shade. It can be used to space out your salon appointments, in turn, saving you money. Another hack I’ve come to love? Using the Style Edit Cover-Up Stick as a quick-fix for sparse brows. This minimizes the number of products floating around your makeup bag, while adding the same definition to your face.

    This is one of those products you didn’t know you needed, but it’ll certainly become the beauty find you tell your friends about. So, secure your Style Edit Instant Root Cover-Up Stick for $36 to join Tomei and yours truly.
