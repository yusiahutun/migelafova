    5 best green coats inspired by Princess Kate's statement outfit

    Looking gorgeous in green, Princess Katevisited Leeds on Tuesday to launcha major new campaignfrom the Royal Foundation Centre for Early Childhood. 'Shaping Us' aims to increase public understanding of the crucial importance of the first years of a childs life.

    26 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Looking gorgeous in green, Princess Kate visited Leeds on Tuesday to launch a major new campaign from the Royal Foundation Centre for Early Childhood. 'Shaping Us' aims to increase public understanding of the crucial importance of the first years of a child’s life. 

    Arriving at Kirkgate Market, the 41-year-old royal was a vision, wearing an emerald Alexander McQueen coat with a matching Manu Atelier handbag, gold hoop earrings by Shyla London, and her trusty Gianvito Rossi brown suede boots.

    Princess Kate wears Alexander McQueen in Leeds

    Kate's stunning outfit comes just weeks after she stepped out in another green Alexander McQueen coat on Christmas Day. She'd previously worn the bespoke designer piece to three separate royal engagements back in 2020.

    Princess Kate wears a green Alexander McQueen coat on Christmas Day
