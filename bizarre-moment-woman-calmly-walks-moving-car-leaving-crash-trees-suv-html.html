    This is the shocking moment a woman stepped out of her moving car on a main road - then walked off as it ploughed into oncoming traffic.

    Jasmine Lacey, 22, was driving an allegedly stolen Hyundai Sonata down a hill through San Bernardino, California, on September 1 at 3.45pm when she came to an abrupt halt.

    She then casually steps out and walks to the center median, leaving the door open and the engine running.

    The Sonata rolls round the bend and across the road, hitting a Honda SUV and a 2003 Lincoln, before crashing into a tree.

    This Hyundai Sonata was seen driving down a hill on September 1 when it suddenly came to a halt

    The driver, identified as 22-year-old Jasmine Lacey, opens the door and proceeds to climb out

    As Lacey is filmed stepping out of the car, she leaves the door open and the engine running

    Video footage of the incident, taken by the vehicle behind Lacey, has amassed more than two million views on ViralHog.com.

    It is not clear why the vehicle's driver had a dash-cam running.
