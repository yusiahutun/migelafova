    2019 Punk Rock Bowling &amp; Music Festival To Feature Rancid, Refused, Descendents, The Specials &amp; More

    LAS VEGAS (CelebrityAccess) – The Punk Rock Bowling & Music Festival has announced its 2019 lineup.

    Scheduled to take place over Memorial Day Weekend, May 24th-27th, 2019, at the Downtown Las Vegas Event Center, this year’s headliners include Rancid, Descendents, The Specials, Refused, The Hives, The Stranglers, and Flag (featuring original members of Black Flag).

    Other acts on the bill include The Damned, The Vandals, FEAR, The Undertones, Perkele, Fucked Up, Shame, Adolescents, Dead Boys, Street Dogs, The Casualties, and more.

    In addition to musical performances, festival-goers will be able to take part in bowling, pool parties, poker tournaments, and more.

    Tickets for the 21st edition of the Punk Rock Bowling & Music Festival are available at PunkRockBowling.com.
