    30 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Yesterday, Channing Tatum put our social media birthday messages to shame when he shared a stunning tribute to his wife on Instagram. The actor and dancer posted a black and white portrait of his spouse, Jenna Dewan—who just turned 37—running her hands through her hair, covered in nothing but pouring water.

    Tatum, 37, shared a heartfelt note to Dewan in his caption:

    "This creature is one more year beautiful. The transformation she invoked this year has been something they only write about in books of fairytale and magic. Your strength and grace ever evolving. By doing so you make everything around you grow- everything. And I am so very grateful for your lessons. Happy life day my love."

    Though Tatum doesn't share too much on Instagram, he isn't afraid to give his wife shoutouts from time to time. For her birthday last year, he called her his "rainbow" and "pot of gold."

    He supported her magazine cover when she fronted an issue of Cosmopolitan.

    He called her his "light."

    And he called her a "magical creature," which may be the only accurate way to describe this woman.

    In addition showering her with compliments on her birthday, Tatum took Dewan on a trip to Mexico over the weekend. The couple visited Cabo San Lucas, where they caught some sun and enjoyed some drinks on the beach.

    Dewan shared plenty of FOMO-inducing scenes from their getaway.

    Dewan and Tatum first met on the set of Step Up in 2006, and ultimately married in 2009. They share a four-year-old daughter named Everly, who loves her Halloween candy and isn't a big fan of her parents' hit movie together.
