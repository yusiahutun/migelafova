    CFDA award-winning designer Telfar Clemens loves a good brand collaboration. From an exclusive release at the start of the pandemic with the late NYC restaurant Mission Chinese, to its highly-anticipated Moose Knuckles capsule that launched earlier this month, the creative has pretty much done it all. Now, in yet another well-matched partnership, the brand is expanding its Ugg collaboration to include an assortment of shearling-lined boots and bags. This time, there's cozy loungewear to match.

    A household name, Ugg is revered for its super soft fabrics and plush footwear. Like Telfar’s cult shopping bag—also known as the “Bushwick Birkin”—the Australian brand’s standard boot has maintained its spot as a must-have item in pretty much everyone’s wardrobe, regardless of the season. Both fanbases get the best of both worlds in a seamless new collection of cozy fleece and shearling staples embossed with the Telfar logo. The collaboration will clearly help you get through winter in style.

    Open Gallery

    Pieces in the lineup include something for everyone. There are a host of furry bags, shearling-lined slippers, and boots stamped with the Telfar logo at the top. Fans can also expect a new gray, sweatpant-like fleece material across categories. Even if you’ve had your fill on cozy loungewear staples, there are plenty of other products in the new collection to choose from.

    The Fall/Winter 2021 Ugg x Telfar collection is available now at select online retailers, including Selfridges.com and Nordstrom.com. Starting November 18, shoppers can also peruse the new pieces at select Ugg boutiques internationally, in addition to destinations like Saks Fifth Avenue and SVRN.
