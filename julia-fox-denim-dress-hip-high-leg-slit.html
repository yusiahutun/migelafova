    Julia Fox delivered yet another boundary-pushing look during Paris Fashion Week.

    At last night's LVMH Prize Cocktail Party, the actress made a statement in an asymmetrical sandblast denim maxi dress. The '90s-esque piece featured a zipper down the middle, a collared neckline, structured baggy long sleeves, a draping skirt, and two dramatic leg slits. On one side, the slit curved over just above her thigh while, on the other side, the slit ended above her hip.

    She continued to up the ante with the rest of her apparel. For footwear, she wore R13's platform tank boots in an earthy dark brown hue. She also matched her accessories to her goth glam, carrying a blood-red Bible bag redesigned by Aylah Gallardo that matched her maroon lip and black cherry hair.

    Rachpoot/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

    Pascal Le Segretain//Getty Images

    The Uncut Gems star has reigned over street style in New York, Milan, and Paris this past month. Her innovative outfits have included assless chaps with an attached horse tail, a sparkling evening gown embroidered with a cartoon poodle, and a completely see-through dress with a belly button piercing cutout.

    Recently, the star has shown a particular affinity for styling belts—as miniskirts, bandeau bras, and minidresses.

    Prior to last night's denim look, Fox continued that experimental trend by stepping out in a minidress made up of black leather belts with large silver buckles. She finished the monochromatic look off with a black leather trench coat trimmed with silver studs, a black mini handbag, and black heeled pumps with ankle lace-ups.
