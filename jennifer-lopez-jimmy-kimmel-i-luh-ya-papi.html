    We luh this!

    Jennifer Lopez stopped by "Jimmy Kimmel Live!" on Monday night, where the "American Idol" judge and the late night host deconstructed the lyrics of her latest single, "I Luh Ya Papi."

    "Luh is short for love," Kimmel explained to his audience. "But it can be confusing, especially for older people who don't know the new lingo, the new abbreviation, so tonight, with Jennifer's help, I'm going to try to translate the meaning of this song in a new segment called, 'New Lyrics For Old People.'"

    Lopez then started going through the lyrics, saying, "I put it down for a brother like you / Give it to you right in the car that's you."

    And Jimmy translated: "Now that means, 'I would like to have sex with you in a motor vehicle.'"

    "That's right!" the brunette beauty said, continuing on, "We can first give you some of this, that's you / And you're all that loving that J.Lo, true."

    "Okay, that, now I don't understand. What does that mean?" Kimmel asked. "That means you love me," she said with a laugh.

    As the two went back and forth, there was plenty of sexual innuendo!
