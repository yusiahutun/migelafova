    Fall is usually that time of year when we start to pile on layer upon layer of cozy knits and long jackets—but Kendall Jenner is here to subvert that sartorial expectation.

    On Sunday, the model and 818 founder left a photo shoot in Los Angeles looking ultra-stylish in a black knit sweater, sheer black Calzedonia tights … and no pants.

    She kept things classy in a pair of pointed black slingbacks and further accessorized with black rectangular sunglasses and a woven top-handle bag from Bottega Veneta. She also toted around a bouquet of bright yellow sunflowers in her arms, adding an extra pop of color to her otherwise monochromatic look.

    The outing came a day before outlets reported that Jenner and her boyfriend of two years, NBA player Devin Booker, broke up for the second time.

    "Both have incredibly busy schedules right now with their careers and they've decided to make that a priority," a source told People, referring to Jenner's packed schedule as an in-demand model and the founder of her 818 tequila brand. Meanwhile, Booker is currently in the midst of playing his eighth NBA season with the Phoenix Suns.

    Another insider added, "They have a lot of love and respect for each other and wish only the best." The outlet noted that the couple mutually decided to end their relationship.

    Jenner and Booker reportedly first broke up this past June. "Kendall feels like they're on different paths," a source told Entertainment Tonight at the time, with another adding that they "have had discussions about their future but they are not on the same page." They reconciled weeks later, only to reportedly split again this month.
