    Akin to figure-sculpting jeans and a sharp blazer, the classic white shirt hides a multitude of style sins. Where instant polish is concerned, there are few pieces more transformative – a crisp, well-fitting shirt will stand you in excellent stead on days when you are pressed for time, not to mention the fact that a flash of white around the neck can give the illusion of a brighter complexion. 

    How to style a white shirt in 2023

    Making up a key part of your sartorial bread and butter, a white shirt is seriously versatile. For capsule wardrobe devotees, it is a total must-have. 

    READ: How to find your personal style according to a fashion expert

    Layer a relaxed-fit linen version over a standout one-piece for pool-lounging purposes, or wear it done up, cinched in at the waist with a rattan belt for maximum holiday style impact.

    For a dose of casual everyday elegance, team your piece with light-wash kick-flare jeans and barely-there sandals, or up the drama by wearing a white shirt open over a lace slip dress and thigh-high boots for night.

    Which white shirt should I buy this season?

    For the ultimate holiday vibes, we adore lightweight styles by the likes of Arket, Mango and Reiss, whereas if a more luxe, tailored feel is on the agenda, Acne Studios, Stella McCartney and The Row boast the best of the white shirts this season.
