    Rihanna Somehow Made a Football Jersey Paired with Clogs Look Good

    Another day, another unconventional outfitcourtesy of beloved street style icon Rihanna. Last night, the Fenty Beauty mogul was spotted heading out for dinner in New York City, where she was accompanied by boyfriend A$AP Rocky.

    18 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Another day, another unconventional outfit—courtesy of beloved street style icon Rihanna.

    Last night, the Fenty Beauty mogul was spotted heading out for dinner in New York City, where she was accompanied by boyfriend A$AP Rocky.

    For the occasion, Rihanna opted for her signature high-low style, going for a sporty casual look in an oversized navy blue jersey and baggy green-and-black checkered pants. She continued the athleisure aesthetic in a pair of black leather clogs from the recent collaboration between Adidas and Gucci. The clogs featured studs along the sides and Gucci's monogram on the heels, while the caps of the shoes bore the house's iconic gold Horsebit and Adidas's logo.

    Accessories included an olive snakeskin bag with a bamboo handle from a Gucci x Tom Ford collaboration and white rectangular sunglasses from Prada.

    Meanwhile, Rocky coordinated with the "Love on the Brain" singer in his own Gucci ensemble. He styled a plain white T-shirt with denim jeans printed all over with the brand's monogram. A pair of black-and-white sneakers, a gold chain necklace, and a black baseball cap finished the look off.

    BACKGRID

    Rihanna and Rocky have been making a few public appearances since they welcomed the arrival of their first child, a baby boy, back in May.

    "They're keeping things low-key so they can enjoy their little family," a source previously told Entertainment Tonight. "Privacy has become increasingly important to the couple. When it was just the two of them, they'd go out all hours of the day and night and interact with fans. Now, with the baby, they're a little more protective."
