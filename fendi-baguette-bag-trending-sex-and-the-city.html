    25 Aug 2024, 00:00

    In September, Fendi hosted the first ever fashion show that was dedicated to a bag, celebrating the 25th anniversary of its iconic Baguette during New York Fashion Week. As part of the celebration, the house revealed a collaboration with actress Sarah Jessica Parker, which was inspired by the sequin-embroidered version of the bag that she made so famous on Sex and the City (and later wore again in And Just Like That...).

    The bags – which were first revealed on the catwalk – have finally launched, meaning you can nab yourself a piece of fashion history, and just in time for Christmas.

    Related Story

    This Baguette is available to shop in four different colours; purple, wasabi green, baby pink and soft turquoise blue – and all feature interchangeable magnetic FF buckles.

    As well as being revealed on the catwalk, Parker was pictured sitting with the purple design on the front row, alongside Kim Kardashian, La La Anthony and Naomi Watts. Parker has had a very long-running relationship with Fendi, and her character Carrie Bradshaw is credited with launching the Fendi Baguette into the It bag it is today.

    Sean Zanni//Getty Images

    Sean Zanni//Getty Images

    Bradshaw's beloved purple sequin Baguette made regular appearances in the original show, and was responsible for such a boom in interest. Parker has previously commented that Fendi was actually one of the first brands to lend products to Sex and the City’s costume designer Patricia Field. "Once Fendi loaned us items, everyone was willing to do so," she writes in Rizzoli’s 2012 Fendi Baguette book. "[It] was a defining moment for us."

    From there, the Fendi Baguette was immortalised as an object to be coveted and carried – the first It bag, faithfully worn by the likes of Paris Hilton, Julia Roberts, Parker (in real life, too) and Elizabeth Taylor (who owned 18), and creating waiting lists at department stores (which was unheard of, at the time).

    Earlier this year, the bag saw a revival again once it debuted on the new series, And Just Like That... According to discount store WeThrift, searches for the bag increased by 1011 per cent.

    If you want to hear more about the fascinating history behind the Fendi Baguette, delve in here.

    The Sarah Jessica Parker x Fendi Baguette in green, pink and blue is available upon reservation in selected Fendi boutiques worldwide from today. The Sarah Jessica Parker x Fendi Baguette in purple will be available as ready to buy at the Roma Palazzo Fendi, London New Bond Street and New York 57th Street boutiques.
