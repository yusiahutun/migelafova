    The 7 Best Apple Watch Apps for Golfers

    Whether you're a weekend warrior or someone who is looking to improve their handicap, technology like the Apple Watch has made it even easier to enjoy golf. Here are the best Apple Watch apps for golfers.

    24 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Whether you're a weekend warrior or someone who is looking to improve their handicap, technology like the Apple Watch has made it even easier to enjoy golf. Here are the best Apple Watch apps for golfers.

    1. 15th Club Golf GPS Rangefinder

    You don’t need to spend a dime to enjoy a great Apple Watch golf app on the course. 15th Club Golf GPS Rangefinder can help you in more ways than one. To get started, you’ll choose the course you are playing on the iPhone version. The app features more than 38,000 courses around the world. Once that’s done, you can put your phone away.

    On the Apple Watch screen, you can see a high-resolution map of each hole. Zoom in to see precise distances to any point on the course including the front, middle, and back of the green. You can also see distances to hazards like a sand trap. Easily keep score with a few taps on the watch screen. It also supports gross and handicap-adjusted scoring.

    The app is also compatible with Apple HealthKit, so a round of golf can help you close your Apple Watch Activity rings.

    Download:15th Club Golf GPS Rangefinder (Free)

    2. Golfshot GPS

    Golfshot GPS is another great option to keep track of your golf rounds on the Apple Watch. After selecting a course and starting a round on your iPhone, you can keep track of the round on the wearable device. For each hole, you can see an aerial overview along with the distances to the front, center, and back of the green. Swipe left to record your score and total putts taken.

    Many features are available with the free version of the app including scoring and course visualization on the Apple Watch. A subscription is needed to unlock additional features like automatic shot tracking with the Apple Watch that allows you to review every shot on the wearable device.

    Download: Golfshot GPS (Free, subscription available)

    3. Hello Birdie

    Hello Birdie is perfect for golfers who a ready to attack the course with as much information as possible available to view on the Apple Watch. All you need to do on the iPhone version is select your course, and then you can start using the Apple Watch app.

    Any golfer knows that it is often hard to decide whether to go for the safe play and layup and try for the green. But on the watch screen, you can choose from either of those options and see a hole map and information on how to proceed. You can even see the current wind direction and speed to make a better choice. Other weather information you can see directly on the watch app include humidity and temperature. There is also a full-featured scorecard component.

    Many of the app’s features are free, but there is an optional subscription. That unlocks a wide variety of additional tools. Some of the highlights include the ability to create a personalized game plan for courses, see plays like lengths, and see a club recommendation, all available to view on the Apple Watch.

    Download:Hello Birdie (Free, subscription available)

    4. Roundabout

    While you’ll need a subscription to make the most use out of Roundabout, there are three different tiers to select from. With the basic, and free option can choose from one of more than 40,000 available courses on the iPhone app. On the Apple Watch, you can then see the length to the front, middle, and back of the green and manually track your score including penalty strokes and putts taken from a selected length.

    The par option will unlock automatic shot tracking. Both the birdie and eagle options unlock additional options just for the iPhone version.

    Download:Roundabout (Free, subscription available)

    5.18Birdies

    Take your scorecard to the next level when using 18Birdies. Just select the course you’re playing on the iPhone to begin. On the main screen of the Apple Watch app, you’ll see GPS information to the hole and green along with your current score, and hole information. Swipe right to enter your score and much more. You can choose whether you hit the green in regulation, fairway hit, number of putts taken, chip shots taken, green side sand shots taken, and penalty strokes. All of that information is saved to your iPhone to access at any time.

    While playing, you can also see a screen with the complete scorecard and round information. All of those features are free. With a subscription, you can unlock many additional features including the ability to view plays like distance on the Apple Watch that takes into account wind and slope-adjusted distances.

    Download:18Birdies (Free, subscription available)

    6. Golf GameBook Scorecard & GPS

    Golf GameBook Scorecard & GPS makes golf social. The app is full of great social features where you can compete against friends and share moments from the round. One of the best features is the ability to use the Apple Watch to create a live scoreboard that can be seen by your friends using the app. You can start a round by yourself or add everyone from a foursome and then keep track of their scores and other information like whether the fairway was hit and the number of putts.

    A subscription is needed to unlock the ability to see GPS-powered distances while on the course on your Apple Watch and other features for the iPhone.

    Download:Golf GameBook Scorecard & GPS (Free, subscription available)

    7. Zepp Golf

    It’s not hard to find gadgets that will help you improve your golf game. One of the most popular options is the Zepp Golf sensor. The sensor attaches to your golf glove and will provide a wealth of information to help improve your golf game like 3D swing analysis, a smart training coach, pro swing library, and more.

    But you don’t need to use the training system to make use of the completely free Zepp Golf Apple Watch app. You can use it while on the range or even during a round. After each swing, you can see tempo, hand path, and hand speed. When your session is complete, you can view fitness metrics along with averages of those three metrics.

    Download:Zepp Golf (Free)

    Enjoy the Course With These Great Apple Watch Golf Apps

    It’s time to ditch the pencil and paper. With these great Apple Watch golf apps, you can jot down your scores, see the distance to the hole, and much more. Fore!
