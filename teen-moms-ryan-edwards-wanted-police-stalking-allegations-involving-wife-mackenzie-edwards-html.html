    Police in Tennessee are looking for former Teen Mom personality Ryan Edwards in connection with allegations he stalked wife Mackenzie Edwards.

    Authorities on Thursday filed the warrants against Edwards, The US Sun reported Monday, citing legal documents, which comes less than two weeks after Edwards, 35, was arrested February 10 in connection with harassment of Mackenzie.

    In his arrest, Edwards - who shares two children with Mackenzie - was also charged with violating an order of protection, and possession of drug paraphernalia and a controlled substance.

    Edwards was subsequently taken into custody and held on a bond of $5,000, the outlet reported. 

    Prior to Edwards' arrest, he took to social media with a picture of Mackenzie topless, captioning the image, 'If you guys have never seen a spineless s*** this is one.'

    Following the social media post, Mackenzie, 26, reported to the police multiple instances of domestic violence Edwards has been involved in since they wed in 2017.

    Mackenzie told police Edwards 'punched holes in the walls and doors' and held her up by her 'neck to the wall' during a fight last month.

    Mackenzie told authorities that Edwards threw her 'down in hallway and said if he could not have me no one could' and 'opened [his] pocket knife' and put it to her 'back like he was going to kill' her.

    She said that the entire incident played out in front of their children, Jagger, four, and Stella, three.

    'I tried to leave, he smashed my phone, took my car keys,' Mackenzie said, adding that a co-worker contacted the authorities in the wake of the incident.

    Mackenzie told police that a week later, Edwards was violent with her again. She said that Edwards 'came into bedroom where [she] was laying down ... pretending like he's going to punch [her] face' while yelling at her.

    She said that after Edwards broke the bed she was lying in at the time, she 'went to get [her] kids and leave,' but he wouldn't let her.
