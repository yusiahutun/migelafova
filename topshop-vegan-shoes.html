    Topshop has today launched its very first vegan shoe collection. The high-street store revealed that this would be its first foray into sustainable, cruelty-free accessories, with more to come.

    "We are really excited to be launching our first vegan collection this April," Maddy Evans, fashion director at Topshop said. "Initially we are focussing on footwear and introducing six new styles that are produced alongside our premium shoe lines in Spain. We can't wait to see how shoppers respond to the design-led collection and how far we can push vegan products across our accessories offering."

    The first vegan collection is made up of six different shoe styles, with each design offered in two colourways. Every sandal is very spring-appropriate – from chunky flats to snake courts and block-heeled sandals, there is something for everyone in the collection.

    The vegan shoes are available from today online and in selected Topshop stores. Prices start from £42.
