    Colleen Wolfe's biography

    Colleen was born on 3rd January 1985 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, in the United States. Her father, Ed Wolfe, was a flight instructor, while her mother, whose name isn't known, was a homemaker. Unfortunately, her parents divorced when she was young. Who is Colleen Wolfe's sister? She has not offered any information about her siblings if she has any.

    After the divorce, Colleen lived with her mother, who raised her single-handedly. After her elementary studies, she joined Hatboro-Horsham Senior High School for her high school studies. After completion, she enrolled at Drexel University, where she majored in Corporate Communications.

    Career beginning

    Colleen began her career right after her university graduation. She landed her first job at the Philadelphia network, WIP working as a morning news anchor and covering sports. She worked in this position for a while, where she honed her skills in production and editing.

    Later, she moved to CN8, where she was appointed to work as a news anchor. She also worked for Comcast SportsNet. She worked behind the scenes as a producer and in front of the camera, delivering sports news.

    In 2012, she joined FOX 29 in Philadelphia, working as a sports reporter. Gradually, she rose to the ranks, with her name gaining global recognition by day. This gave her more job opportunities, such as PHL17 as a producer. Later, she worked on the Golf Show airing on the CSN channel.
