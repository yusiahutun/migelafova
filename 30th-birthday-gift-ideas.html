    30th birthday gifts I want for myself…

      There's no denying that turning 30 can stir up some emotions. It's a huge milestone and cause for celebration, but it's only natural to feel a little bit of anxiety about waving goodbye to your 20s.  

      Luckily, if you have a loved one turning 30, I have some 30th birthday gift ideas that will definitely soften the blow.

      From stylish homeware to designer fashion pieces and luxe skincare sets, there are so many options I know she would appreciate. This includes things that start to excite you so much more as you move into your fourth decade. Case in point? Stationery, candles and coffee table books.

      Whatever her taste, I've found all of the best 30th birthday gift ideas to shop online now that will satisfy even the fussiest recipient. Scroll on to shop the edit.

      The best 30th birthday gifts at a glance

      The celebrity-favourite jewellery: Monica Vinader Alta Capture Charm Bracelet, £295 / $450

      The designer bag: Saint Laurent Le 5 à 7 Mini Leather Shoulder Bag, £1,295 / $1,750

      The personalised champagne: Moët & Chandon Personalised Champagne, £76.99 / $85

      The coffee table book: Assouline Ibiza Bohemia Photography Book, £85 / $107

      The fragrance: Creed Carmina Eau de Parfum, £165 / $300

      The skincare set: Augustinus Bader The Hydration Heroes, £235 / $325

      How I chose the best 30th birthday gifts for her

      • Expert-approved: I turned 30 a couple of years ago and these are all gifts my friends and I would have loved to receive.
      • Variety: Of course everyone's different, so I've included a range of item categories from beauty and hair tools to homeware and stationery.
      • Price: In this edit you'll find everything from the super pricey to gifts suitable for a smaller budget.
