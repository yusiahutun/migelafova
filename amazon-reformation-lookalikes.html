    Like Olivia Rodrigo, Taylor Swift, and Kendall Jenner, we love Reformation’s clothing, shoes, and accessories. However, the brand isn’t always budget-friendly, and before we spend big on basics, we like to browse other retailers for similar styles. There are tons of Reformation lookalikes to shop on Amazon, for instance, and if you don’t mind swapping the real thing for an alternative, it’s possible to get the same great look for a fraction of the price.

    Of course, we wouldn’t just tell you this without some cold, hard proof. Ahead, we’re giving you options: the Reformation alternatives from Amazon that we think are worth buying, along with links to the actual product.

    Plus, if you still want to shop the brand but are hoping to spend a little less, you’re in luck. Reformation’s Winter Sale is still happening and tons of items are heavily discounted right now.

    • ANRABESS Women Satin Maxi Dress

      Fans of Reformation know that it’s the place to shop for wedding guest dresses. However, if the Kailyn Silk Dress is a tad over what you’re willing to spend, the reviews for this similar, sleeveless satin number say the material is pretty thick and the fit is super flattering.

      Sizes Available: S – XL

    • WDIRARA Women's Split Hem Mini Skirt

      If you ask us, no closet is complete without a versatile black mini. Whether you style it with an oversized sweater or a crisp white button-down, this $30.99 option is just as cute as Reformation’s $98 Margot Skirt — and offers a few more size options, too.

      Sizes Available: XXS – 4X

    • Tronjori Women High Waist Casual Straight Leg Pant

      If you’re not in the mood for a stiff pair of jeans but aren’t willing to step out in sweats, make a loose pair of trousers your new go-to pants. This neutral gray pair can be dressed up or down and is nearly identical to Reformation’s Mason Pant.

      Sizes Available: 4 – 16

    • Epsion Hobo Shoulder Bag

      Why not kick off 2024 with a brand-new bag? Swap out your little black purse for this eye-catching, buckle-embellished one . Not only does it give off ’90s and Y2K vibes, it’s also comparable to the Rafaella Shoulder Bag.

    • Aerosoles Women's Micah Knee High Boot

      Recently, Taylor Swift stepped out wearing Reformation’s $478 River Knee Boot, pairing the sleek, ruby-red style with a plaid skirt and a shearling-trimmed jacket. However, it’s now possible to copy the singer’s look for less than half the price, thanks to Aerosoles’ faux leather alternative .
