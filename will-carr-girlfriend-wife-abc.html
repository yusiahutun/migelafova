    News correspondent Will Carr is one handsome face we see serving in the journalism field. And, his love life has been quite interesting too. Without further ado, let's get to know more about the ABC news reporter.

    Will Carr Dating Girlfriend Brianna Ruffalo

    News reporter Will Carr who isn't scared to do challenging tasks has worked hard to earn the career he has today. Relationship and love matters have been great for the journalist too, to say the least.

    Will Carr and Brianna Ruffalo met while working for ABC. SOURCE: Instagram

    Speaking of which, Carr is officially off the market and is taken by none other than his beautiful colleague, Brianna Ruffalo who serves as a weather forecaster for the news station. Needless to say, Carr met his girlfriend after he began working for ABC.

    Furthermore, Will and his girlfriend Brianna have been dating for over two years since they publicized their relationship on social media in early 2019. As of March 2021, Carr and Ruffalo are still going stronger with their relationship.

    Not to mention, Carr also often expresses his love for his boo on his Instagram page on special occasions like birthday and Valentine's Day. On Ruffalo's birthday in 2020, Carr shared a birthday picture of the two celebrating and mentioned Ruffalo as his gorgeous partner in crime.

    Will Carr celebrating his girlfriend Brianna Ruffalo's birthday. SOURCE: Instagram

    And, that definitely tells Carr and his girlfriend share a deep bonding and love. But, Will is yet to pop the question to Brianna. Carr and Ruffalo are not engaged nor married, but the lovebirds are definitely having the time of their lives.

    Will Carr Previously Dated Marci Gonzalez

    Meanwhile, Will Carr's relationship with Brianna Ruffalo is not the only one that's publicly known as he previously dated fellow news reporter Marci Gonzalez. Well, Carr and Marci who is also a news reporter for ABC share a history.

    Will Carr and former girlfriend Marci Gonzalez. SOURCE: Pinterest

    Will was in a relationship with Marci from mid-2016 and the two went on to be together for around a year. But, by 2018, Carr and his ex-girlfriend split and went their separate ways.

    As Carr moved on and found love in Ruffalo, Gonzalez went on to date fellow ABC7 news anchor Jory Rand in 2018. As a matter of fact, Carr's ex and her new partner also share a child together a son named Brooks Henry Rand, the couple welcomed in May 2020.

    ABC News reporter Marci Gonzalez shares a son with boyfriend Jory Rand. SOURCE: Instagram

    In addition to Carr's personal life, he grew up with three sisters as the eldest child of his parents. Not to mention, his father serves as an attorney. The award-winning news reporter who received the New Mexico Broadcaster's Association Reporter of the Year 2011 also speaks Spanish and was a footballer until he changed his major in college.

    Carr then graduated with a degree in Broadcast Journalism from the University of Georgia in 2006. Aside from ABC, Carr has also worked for Fox News channel, WTVC-TV News Channel 9, and KOAT Action 7 News. He currently lives in Los Angeles with his girlfriend.
