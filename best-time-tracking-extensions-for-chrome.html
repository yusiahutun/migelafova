    The 5 Best Time Tracking Extensions for Google Chrome

    03 Apr 2024, 00:00

    We all have the same number of hours in a day, and in an attempt to boost your productivity, you might be tempted to extend your work hours. But, as you might know by now, that’s not always the best solution.

    Consider improving your focus during work hours instead. Tracking your time is a practical way to find out where you spend most of your time.

    A Case for Chrome Time Tracker Extensions

    Google Chrome extensions, in general, provide you with extra features and functionality, allowing you to do more with the browser. You can, therefore, use them to customize your Chrome experience and boost your productivity.

    Time tracking extensions for Chrome let you do just that. They let you see where you spend most of your time, while giving you insights into your work patterns. You can leverage this knowledge to make informed decisions about using your time better.

    You can use time tracking extensions for a variety of purposes. For instance, if you’re a freelancer using Google Chrome, you can track your time on each project and send invoices to your clients accordingly. They also let you eliminate the boring, repetitive, and manual process of filling out timesheets.

    Last but not least, if you are spending a good chunk of your working hours on Chrome, using these extensions to track your time, as opposed to standalone time tracking apps, can be more convenient and efficient, as you don’t have to switch between apps.

    Ideally, you should select a Google Chrome extension that's easy to use, works across different devices, and integrates with some of the tools in your productivity toolkit. With this in mind, let’s look at some of the best time tracking extensions for Chrome that you can use.

    The 5 Best Google Chrome Time Tracking Extensions

    1. RescueTime

    RescueTime is a popular time tracking tool with a Chrome extension that tracks your time spent on different websites while using Google Chrome.

    Once you install the extension and sign up, it runs in the background and automatically tracks your activity. It then gives you detailed reports, showing how you spend your time. This extension automatically stops tracking your time when you’re inactive for a few minutes, allowing you to avoid inaccurate results.

    If you upgrade to its premium account, you will be able to leverage the FocusTime feature to block distractions and stay productive. You can also set reminders and goals, and get real-time alerts if you’re spending too much time on non-work-related activities.

    Lastly, your team can also use RescueTime to see how everyone is spending their time and where there might be opportunities for improvement.

    Download: RescueTime for Chrome (Free, premium version available)

    2. Hubstaff Time Tracker

    Hubstaff time tracker is another excellent Chrome extension for tracking your time and that of your team. It runs in the background when you choose a project to work on and start the timer.

    The extension will track your time while working on that project. You can then generate comprehensive reports showing how much time you spend on each task, allowing you to make more accurate estimates on future projects.

    Hubstaff tracks other data points, such as keyboard and mouse activity, which gives you a more accurate picture of your time. It integrates with other popular tools like Trello, Asana, and Jira, to name a few.

    Download: Hubstaff Time Tracker for Chrome (Free, premium version available)

    3. TimeCamp

    TimeCamp is a time tracking tool with a Chrome extension that helps you regain control over your time.

    After adding this Google Chrome extension to your browser, you can track your time while you work on different websites. You can use it to assess your team’s productivity, track time on specific projects, and measure their profitability. It then provides you with an insightful report to help you better manage your time and other resources.

    Several features make this tool ideal for teams, including overtime tracking, payroll automation, attendance tracking, and more. TimeCamp integrates with tools like Asana, Trello, and Slack, saving you time and the need to switch between apps.

    Download: TimeCamp for Chrome (Free, premium version available)

    4. Clockify

    Clockify is one of the most well-known time tracking and timesheet tools. It offers a Chrome extension that gives you a broad view of how you spend your time.

    You can use Clockify to plan and schedule projects with your team, track time on specific tasks, request and approve leaves, and record project expenses, to name a few. It also doubles as an invoicing tool, allowing you to create invoices from your time logs quickly.

    After adding your team members, Clockify will keep track of how everyone is spending their time, and you can also use its calendar feature to plan and manage projects. You can connect Clockify to your Asana, Trello, Jira, and Google Calendar account for added convenience.

    Download: Clockify for Chrome (Free, premium version available)

    5. actiTIME

    actTIME is a time tracking software solution packed with features for individuals and businesses alike, including project tracking, time tracking, billing and invoicing, and reports and data analyses.

    Once you have set up your account, you can start by adding a task and setting a deadline. The Chrome extension will then start tracking your time, and you can stop the timer at any time.

    actiTIME allows you to evaluate employee performance reviews, as it tracks how much time team members spend on specific tasks. Its detailed financial and time tracking report can empower you to make better business decisions.

    On the whole, this tool provides you with the insights you need to better plan your projects, measure your team’s productivity, and bill clients accurately.

    Download: actiTIME for Chrome (Free trial, premium version available)

    Regain Control Over Your Time to Maximize Your Productivity

    Time tracking Chrome extensions can help you and your team regain control over how you spend your time and become more productive as a result. They are packed with features to help you track time on specific tasks, plan and schedule projects, and invoice clients accurately.

    So, if you’re looking for a way to improve your workflow, then consider trying one of these time tracking extensions.
