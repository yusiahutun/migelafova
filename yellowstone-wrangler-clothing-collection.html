    Yellowstone is the most popular show on television, and it only makes sense that its legions of devoted fans want to look the part. After all, the Paramount Network hit has inspired a Westerncore trend inspired by the looks of Beth Dutton, Rip Wheeler, and the rest of the characters. Wrangler is here to add some authentic fashion to your wardrobe with their new Yellowstone collection.

    Wrangler

    It should come as no surprise that the Western lifestyle brand is a perfect match for Yellowstone. Holly Wheeler, vice president of global marketing for Wrangler, put it this way: "Inspired by the hit Paramount ranching series, the newest drop of Wrangler x Yellowstone encourages western enthusiasts to truly dress like a Dutton with styles nodding to everyone’s favorite Yellowstone characters. We've woven the true spirit of the West into our designs and have further integrated cowboy-core style into mainstream fashion by collaborating with a juggernaut in the western space like Yellowstone."

    All of the styles include either the name of beloved Dutton Ranch, the logo, or even a saying about the show. The line starts at $24, with jackets costing up to $89. If you want one, grab them while they last! These styles will sell out fast.
