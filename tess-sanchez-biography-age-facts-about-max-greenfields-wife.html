    Apart from those who make their living in Hollywood, not many people outside the world of filmmaking would take the time to learn more about those who work behind the scenes, such as casting directors. However, there will be an exception if your work speaks for you or if you have a romantic relationship with an actress or actor, as is the case with Tess Sanchez. Read on to learn more about her work and other facts about her.

    Tess Sanchez – Biography (Age)

    There are contradictory reports about the exact year of the birth of Tess Sanchez. A few publications report that she was born in 1984, while others set the date in 1980. All publications seem to agree that she is an American woman born on September 4. Information about her parents and whether or not she has siblings is not available.

    As with details about her family, Sanchez’s early educational history is not known. As for her university education, however, her LinkedIn profile says that she attended Woodbury University, a private institution in Southern California. The profile also states that she studied economics, management, marketing, and related support services and graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree.

    While the exact year she graduated is not known, Tess Sanchez quickly began a career in Hollywood, where she worked as an independent casting director. She held this role for a time before being appointed Vice President of Talent and Casting for the WB Television Network. Sanchez held this position for eight years, from January 2000 to January 2008, before joining Fox Broadcasting Company. During her time at The WB, she helped cast the talent that appeared on shows such as Smallville, Supernatural, and Dawson’s Creek.

    After two years as part of Fox, Sanchez’s work was especially appreciated by her bosses as she was appointed head of the station’s casting unit, making her one of the youngest executives at a major broadcaster. In her role, the Casting Director helped cast the talent that appeared on shows such as Empire, Gotham, Wayward Pines, and Brooklyn Nine-Nine.

    Other Facts about Max Greenfield’s Wife

    Family – Marriage to Max Greenfield

    Tess Sanchez has been married to actor Max Greenfield since 2008. The couple had been together for a while before that, as it was reported that they got engaged on their five-year anniversary before later exchanging vows. Details of the couple’s wedding, as well as some other aspects of their marriage, are not known to the public.

    What is known, however, is that Sanchez is the one who takes care of the couple’s home by “guiding” their Golden Globe and Emmy Award-nominated husband and children in what to do at home. Greenfield, who in addition to his role in New Girl is best known for his recurring roles in Veronica Mars and Ugly Betty, as well as his role as Roger in the Ice Age franchise, revealed this in an interview with Elle Magazine.

    Two years after signing the Covenant for Life in 2008, Sanchez and Greenfield added a new member to their family when they welcomed their first child, a daughter named Lily Greenfield, who was born on January 27, 2010. Five years later, the family grew again when Sanchez gave birth to a son named Ozzie James Greenfield, born August 24, 2015.

    Net Worth and Salary

    Tess Sanchez has undoubtedly lived well from her career as a casting director for one of the largest television stations in the United States. While the exact figures of both her net worth and salary are unknown, the Bureau of Labour Statistics Occupational Employment and Lages has found that casting directors earn an average salary of $70,660 per year. The report also shows that the top earners in this profession, to which Sanchez definitely belongs, take home about $187,000 per year, while the lowest earners take home about $32,010 per year.
