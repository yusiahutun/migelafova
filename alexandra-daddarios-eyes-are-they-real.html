    Who Is Alexandra Daddario?

    Alexandra Anna Daddario is a famous Hollywood actress of Italian, Irish, English, and Czech descent. She was born in New York City on March 16, 1986, to parents Christina and Richard Daddario.

    Her mother is a lawyer, while her father is a prosecutor and former head of the New York City Police Department counterterrorism unit. Her paternal grandfather Emilio Q. Daddario is famous for being a Democratic member of the US House of Representatives for Connecticut from 1959 to 1971. 

    Alexandra has two siblings, Matthew and Catharine, who are also actors in the movie industry. She and her siblings grew up together and were raised on Manhattan’s Upper East Side. The young actress attended Brearley School and the Professional Children’s School, and her interest in acting began at 11.

    Furthering her education, she attended Marymount Manhattan College before dropping out to pursue acting full-time. She also continued studying and working at the Meisner acting technique school. 

    Alexandra made her television debut at 15 when she played victimized girl Laurie Lewis in the ABC soap opera “All My Children.” Her first significant role was in the critically acclaimed “Percy Jackson & the Olympians: The Lightning Thief” playing Annabeth Chase in 2010. 

    In 2012, Alexandra took on a leading role in the music video for Imagine Dragons’ hit song “Radioactive,” which accumulated over 1 billion views on YouTube. Over the years, she has featured in various movies and become a Hollywood household name. Alexandra has also been nominated for the industry’s most prestigious awards and won a couple of them. 

    Are Alexandra Daddario’s Eyes Real?

    However, Alexandra Daddario’s eyes are 100% real, and the “True Detective” actress has mentioned this several times. In an interview with We Got This Covered, she confirmed that her eyes were honest and revealed that they are hereditary.

    She said, “My grandfather had bright blue eyes, and it’s funny because he’s passed away, but people still talk about his eyes. People who knew him always bring it up.”

    While most people adore her eyes, others find it very “creepy and unnerving.” This might be because her eyes are large and expressive and can communicate various emotions to draw the viewer in. Her gaze can be quite disarming with her thick and luxurious lashes.
