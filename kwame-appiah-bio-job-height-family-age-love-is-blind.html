    The first five episodes of Love Is Blind Season 4 are now live on Netflix beginning on March 24th. For the first time in Love Is Blind history, the show is hitting the west coast. As a result of which, Season 4 of LIB has an interesting set of contestants.

    Kwame Appiah is one of the 30 contestants in Love Is Blind Season 4, who shared his excitement about appearing on the popular Netflix show via an Instagram post on March 7th. Will he be able to find the one? Find that out and a lot more about Kwame, including his age, job, family, and more in the wiki article below.

    Love Is Blind: Kwame Appiah And Chelsea Griffin Still Together?

    Love Is Blind is such an intriguing show, primarily for the reason that all the couples date and get engaged before meeting each other in person. It’s always interesting to see how couples react while seeing each other in person for the first time and how their reactions impact their relationships.

    LIB Season 4’s couple, Kwame Appiah and Chelsea Griffin had a time together full of ups and downs.

    While Kwame had found conversations with Chelsea interesting from the start, he also went on dates with Micah Lussier. Struck by a terrible dilemma, Kwame was stunned yet relieved when Micah told him that she wanted them to explore other connections.

    Later, when Kwame learned that Micah was exclusively dating Paul Peden, he was far from happy. Meanwhile, on the other end, when Chelsea learned about the development of Kwame and Micah’s relationship, she was happy that the “charming, sexy momma’s boy” of her dreams would finally focus on her.

    The connection between Kwame and Chelsea strengthened with every date they were having with each other. Eventually, Kwame proposed to Chelsea, and the two got engaged.

    When the two met each other for the first time, the feeling was common: delightful. Kwame and Chelsea also had a wonderful time spending time in Mexico after their engagement.

    However, when all the couples returned from their trips and saw each other in person, Kwame could not help but think about his time with Micah at the pods. Micah also seemed to like Kwame, and the two ended up talking with each other for a long time, something which irritated Chelsea.

    Frustrated by Kwame’s intimacy with Micah, Chelsea had a big argument with her fiance. Yet, the couple decided to stay together. Eventually, Kwame’s interaction with Micah reduced and that helped Kwame and Chelsea revitalize their love for each other.

    Despite the minor hiccups, the duo is still together in real life. Though, it does not seem like their troubles are ending anytime soon. It will be interesting to see how Kwame and Chelsea tackle issues that may come up in their day-to-day lives.

    What Is Kwame Appiah Ideal Girlfriend?

    Apparently, before Kwame applied for Love Is Blind, he was single for four years. Kwame said compatibility has been tough to find for him.

    Coming into the show, Kwame said he’s over the “meet up, try it out, fail and try again” cycle of modern dating and ready to find an ideal partner that “matches my energy” and can join Thanksgiving dinner with his family.

    Through an Instagram post on March 31, 2019, Kwame also revealed that actress Lupita Nyongo is his celebrity crush. He wrote, “… All I want for my birthday is a date with @lupitanyongo, it’ll happen someday I’m sure… .”

    Kwame Appiah Age

    Reportedly, Kwame’s age was 32 as of March 2023.

    Kwame Appiah Family

    Kwame Appiah’s brother is Jerry Appiah. According to Jerry’s Facebook, he studied at the University of Maryland, College Park, and Quince Orchard High School. Jerry is married to Lacey Appiah, who works as a real estate agent. They also have a baby together.

    On May 18, 2018, Kwame took to Instagram to share a picture of his newborn nephew. “… My first nephew was born only several weeks ago. Congratulations to my brother on the baby, and the family for a new KING!,” he captioned the post.

    Some of Kwame’s other family members include Joe, Kobi, and Odeneho Mayele Appiah.

    Is Kwame Appiah On Instagram?

    You can find Kwame Appiah on Instagram @ayokwam, where he has close to 3.5 thousand followers. Additionally, he is also on Facebook.

    Kwame Appiah Height

    LIB star Kwame Appiah stands at an average height of 5 feet 10 inches.

    Kwame Appiah Job

    Talking about Kwame’s job, he has been working as Head of Community Development at Common Room since August 2022. Prior to that, he was Head of Business Development at Builder.io. From September 2019 to February 2022, Kwame also worked at ZoomInfo.

    Furthermore, Kwame is an excellent photographer too. You can check out some of his portraits by clicking here.

    Kwame did his Bachelor’s Studies in Marketing at Goldey-Beacom College. And from 2015 to 2017, he studied at Goldey-Beacom College for his MBA in Business Administration and Management.

    Coming to his salary, we don’t the exact figures yet. However, as per data by salary.com, the average remuneration for a Chief Community Development Officer is around $184 thousand.
