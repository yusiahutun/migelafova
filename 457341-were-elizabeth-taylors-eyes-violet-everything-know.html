    Were Elizabeth Taylor's eyes violet?

    Elizabeth was primarily remembered for her passionate performance in several movies and television series. However, one thing about her also stood out - Elizabeth Taylor's purple eyes. But were they real?

    Elizabeth Taylor eyes close up pictures reveal that they were not violet. However, the violet hue could be attributed to her make-up or the kind of dress code she chose. Usually, make-up can be used to bring out specific colours. She was often photographed wearing blue or purple eyeshadow.

    Furthermore, the appearance of the iris depends on the natural pigment melanin it contains. Your genes determine the melanin levels. The more melanin in your iris, the darker your eyes will look.

    Elizabeth's eyes had a very specific and rare amount of melanin. According to Norman Saffra, there are various shades of blues and greys, with many in-between. Therefore, violet may have been Elizabeth's typical pigmentation.
