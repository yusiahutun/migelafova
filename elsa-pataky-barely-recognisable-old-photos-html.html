    One retro snap shows the Snakes On A Plane actress with a 'Rachael' style haircut and a darker shade of blonde.

    The aesthetic is a far cry from Elsa's current Bohemian style that she's now famous for.

    Taking the plunge: Despite the 40-year-old's wardrobe changing dramatically over the years, she appears to have always been a fan of low-cut fashion to show off her cleavage (pictured in 2001 on left and 2006 on right)

    Power couple: The Spanish actress has become a local star after relocating to Byron Bay with her Aussie husband Chris Hemsworth in 2014

    Meanwhile, the fit mum appears to have always been a fan of low-cut fashion to flaunt her cleavage.

    Since relocating to Byron Bay with her husband and their three children - India Rose, four, and twins Tristan and Sasha, two - in 2014, the former model has become well-known for her healthy and wholesome lifestyle.

    Last year, the fitness book author revealed her secret to maintaining her youthful appearance and glowing complexion.

    Fit mama: The mother-of-three often shares her tips for staying fit and healthy with her 1.2million Instagram followers as well as on her blog (left: 2010, right: 2005)

    Sheer daring: Elsa's red carpet style has certainly evolved since her debut in the Fast & Furious franchise in 2011 (pictured in 2004)

    The petite star told OK! magazine her body is fuelled by a healthy green juice, which she makes at home.

    'An apple, a carrot, some celery with the green leaves, a lemon without the peel,' she revealed the ingredients.

    Continuing with the recipe, she noted: 'Once it's done, you add a few teaspoons of olive oil. Super good.'

     Age-defying beauty: The fitness enthusiast's laidback lifestyle in Byron Bay has certainly paid off, with the star appearing to have maintained her youthful glow (left: age 27, right: age 39)

    Glowing: The stunner showed off her glowing complexion and new hairstyle at a Botanicals Fresh Care event on Wednesday

    Based near Seven Mile Beach in a lavish $7million home with her family, Elsa often shares workout snaps and tips with her 1.2million Instagram followers.

    From surfing and yoga to HIIT circuits and boxing, the busy mum is determined to stay active despite hectic work schedules. 

    Born in Madrid, the mother-of-three was a TV and theatre star in her native Spain before her big Hollywood break.

    The blonde beauty made her debut in the Fast & Furious franchise in 2011, which propelled her to mainstream fame.
