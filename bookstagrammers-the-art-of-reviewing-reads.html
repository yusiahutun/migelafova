    Bookstagrammers: The Art of Reviewing and Recommending Reads

    Bookstagrammers are book lovers who share their love of reading on Instagram. They post photos and videos of the books they’re reading, reviewing, and recommending. Bookstagrammers are a passionate and engaged community, and their recommendations can have a big impact on what people read.           

    In this article, we’ll take a look at the world of bookstagramming and explore the art of reviewing and recommending reads. We’ll also share some tips on how to find and follow the best bookstagrammers for your interests.    

    What is bookstagramming?

    Bookstagramming is a social media phenomenon that combines the love of books and the power of Instagram. Bookstagrammers share their love of reading on Instagram by posting photos and videos of the books they’re reading and reviewing.   

    (Source-publishdrive.com)

    Bookstagrammers come from all walks of life, but they all have a passion for reading. They may be students, teachers, librarians, booksellers, or simply avid readers. What unites them is their love of sharing books with others.

    Bookstagrammers use their Instagram accounts to share their thoughts on books, recommend new books to their followers, and connect with other book lovers. They may also post photos and videos of their book collections, book hauls, and reading spaces.    

    The art of reviewing and recommending reads

    Bookstagrammers are not just book enthusiasts; they are also skilled reviewers and recommenders. They take the time to read books carefully and write thoughtful and engaging reviews. They also consider their followers’ interests when recommending books.

    When writing a review, bookstagrammers typically discuss the following elements: 

    • Plot: What is the story about?
    • Characters: Who are the main characters and how are they developed?
    • Writing style: How does the author write? Is the writing clear, concise, and engaging?
    • Pace: Is the book well-paced, or does it drag at times?
    • Overall thoughts: What did the bookstagrammer think of the book overall? Would they recommend it to others?

    Bookstagrammers also use their reviews to discuss the themes and messages of the books they read. They may also compare and contrast books to other similar books.

    How to find and follow the best bookstagrammers?

    If you’re new to bookstagramming, there are a few things you can do to find and follow the best bookstagrammers: 

    (Source-GETTY)

    • Use hashtags: Hashtags are a great way to find bookstagrammers who share your interests. Some popular bookish hashtags include #bookstagram, #books, #reading, #bookworm, and more. 
    • Follow book publishers and authors: Many book publishers and authors have their own Instagram accounts where they share news about their books and interact with readers. Following these accounts is a great way to discover new bookstagrammers.
    • Explore bookstagram communities: There are many online communities for bookstagrammers. These communities are a great place to find new bookstagrammers to follow and to connect with other book lovers.

    Once you’ve found some bookstagrammers you’re interested in, be sure to follow them and engage with their content. Like their posts, leave comments, and ask questions. This is a great way to get to know them better and learn more about their book recommendations.  

    Tips for becoming a bookstagrammer

    If you’re interested in becoming a bookstagrammer, here are a few tips:  

    • Be genuine: Be yourself and share your genuine love of books. People can sense when you’re not being genuine, so it’s crucial to be yourself.  
    • Take good photos: Bookstagram is a visual platform, so it’s important to take good photos of your books. Use good lighting and composition, and don’t be afraid to experiment.
    • Write engaging reviews: Your reviews should be well-written and informative. Share your thoughts and feelings about the book, and explain why you would or would not recommend it to others.
    • Interact with your followers: Respond to comments and questions from your followers. This is a great way to build a community and connect with other book lovers.

    Conclusion

    Bookstagrammers are a passionate and engaged community of book lovers who share their love of reading with others on Instagram. They are skilled reviewers and recommenders, and their recommendations can have a significant impact on what people read.
