How to Shop Coachtopia by Coach Bags 2023

13 Sep 2024, 00:00

Coach believes in leaving no scrap behind. The colorful patchwork shoulder bags and other covetable artisanal designs in the American leather goods house’s new Coachtopia sub-brand are literally made with scraps from the cutting-room floor. (More specifically, the small and irregular scraps of leather left on the cutting-room floor where Coach bag patterns are trimmed from hides that would normally wind up in landfills.) This different and disruptive thinking is the latest and most expansive example of the Coach’s efforts over the past three years to lower its carbon footprint, which have included incorporating embroidered and embellished upcycled vintage clothing into ins runway collections and introducing a (Re)Loved line of refurbished vintage and recent bags.
