    12 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Brought in by Islander Molly-Mae Hague, the sweet stuffed toy elephant has been one of the surprise stars of this year’s Love Island.

    But viewers were stunned to discover something unexpected about Ellie-Belly on last night’s finale – to their surprise, the toy was a boy, rather than a girl.

    Throughout her relationship with boxer Tommy Fury, Ellie-Belly has stayed by her side, with the sweethearts jokingly referring to it as their “baby”.

    When Tommy asked her to be his girlfriend, he used the teddy to help him pop the question and would often wake up with the stuffed toy planted on his head.

    Playing such a major role in their relationship, it only made sense that they take Ellie-Belly with them into the final.

    The 19-year-old beauty sat the miniature elephant on her lap as she chatted with Caroline Flack on the sofa.

    Holding up the toy, she said: "I had a little chat with Ellie-Belly earlier and he said he never expected to make the final.”

    Once viewers heard how she had described the teddy, they were utterly floored, having assumed that Ellie-Belly was a female all along.

    One said: “Hold up! Ellie-Belly is a boy?"

    A second exclaimed: “Wait Ellie-Belly is a... boy? That’s the biggest shock of this series.”

    While a third remarked: “How the f*** is Ellie-Belly a boy?”

    Now that they’ve left the villa in second place, Molly-Mae and Tommy will have to go head-to-head to see who gets to take Ellie-Belly home with them.

    That said, if their plans to move in together go ahead, they won’t have to fight over him after all.
