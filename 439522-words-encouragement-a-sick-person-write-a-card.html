    What to write in a get well soon card

    A well-wish card can brighten an otherwise crummy day when someone is seriously ill or just a bit under the weather. Here is what to write in a get well card.

    • Being at the hospital made me realise how much you help me be myself and keep me happy by being by my side. I miss you so much! Get well soon, please.
    • May God send you angels to watch over you as you recover. I pray that you get well soon.
    • Forget all work and take adequate rest as prescribed by your doctor. This will help you to recover soon. We are here to help you out anytime you need to.
    • Close your eyes and think about the blessings of your life. Celebrate the goodness of the Lord. You are sick, yet you still breathe. God will heal you soon.
    • You may be under the weather, but at least you’re not under a bus! Feel better!
    • You’re so brave, and I’m so proud of your positivity during this challenging time. You’ve got this!
    • You will never find a more effective medicine than hope. So, always be hopeful that God will get you recovered from this sickness in no time. Get well soon!
    • I pray that God heals your pain and suffering. He will help you to feel better soon.
    • Get well soon! I hope you recover from your ailment without facing any complications. You will be in my prayers!
    • Take this token from me, a basket full of love and happiness from someone who cares very much about you. I wish you fast healing from your illness and sound health for all the years to come. Please get well soon!
    • Know that God gives us strength when we most need it, and I pray that you find strength and peace during this challenging time.
    • Your cheerful smile is being greatly missed, love! I hope you heal from your pain soon!
    • Don’t forget that you are a strong person who can efficiently deal with minor injuries. I wish you a fit and healthy life soon.
    • Nothing is permanent in this world; this tough time will pass soon. Therefore, I will pray to the Almighty God to bring you comfort and good health.
    • Concentrating on work without you beside me is getting hard for me. Get well soon, love. I cannot wait to see you.
