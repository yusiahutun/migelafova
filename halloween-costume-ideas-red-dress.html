    5 Halloween Costumes That Require Only a Red Dress

    Bella Hadid impersonated Lydia Deetz from Tim Burton's 1988 Beetlejuice film with a frothy bloodred tulle gown, layered over a polka-dot sheer blouse. Ace this look by teasing your hair and adding a bouquet of dead red roses.

    Bella Hadid as Lydia Deetz

    Gilbert Carrasquillo//Getty Images

    Bella Hadid impersonated Lydia Deetz from Tim Burton's 1988 Beetlejuice film with a frothy bloodred tulle gown, layered over a polka-dot sheer blouse. Ace this look by teasing your hair and adding a bouquet of dead red roses.

    Loulou Floral Appliqué Tulle Dress

    Loulou Floral Appliqué Tulle Dress

    The Loulou's dress is the perfect fashionable yet costume-worthy piece to include in your Lydia ensemble. 

    Heidi Klum as Betty Boop

    Rose Hartman//Getty Images

    Supermodel Heidi Klum adores Halloween, and she throws the most lavish celebrity-filled every year. In 2002, she showed up as the ever-popular flapper girl, Betty Boop, a powerful symbol of bold sexuality.

    Rhode Molly cutout-back cotton-poplin dress

    Short and sweet, with just enough coverage, Rhode's cotton-poplin dress is cute enough to be worn well after the festivities cease.
