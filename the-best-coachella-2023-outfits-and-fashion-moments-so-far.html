    The Coachella Valley Music And Arts Festival is upon us once more, and the glitterati are pulling out all the stops for the occasion. 

    A galaxy of stars have already descended on the lush fields to watch the headliners and perform. Supermodels such as Alessandra Ambrosio, Stella Maxwell and Irina Shayk have been spotted, as well as the world's most renown musical acts including Billie Eilish and Blackpink.

    When it comes to festival style, the event, which is held annually at the Empire Polo Club in Indio, California is one of the most hotly anticipated on the sartorial calendars alongside Burning Man and Glastonbury.

    The location is roughly a two-hour drive from Los Angeles, meaning that it guarantees an A-list guest list. After a long Covid-19 induced hiatus this is set to be a bumper year for Coachella style. We take a look at the standout fashion moments from Coachella 2023 (so far)…

    The best outfits at Coachella 2023:

    Amelia Dimoldenberg:

    The Chicken Shop Date's Amelia Dimoldenberg attended Coachella's Marc Jacobs & i-D 'Pre-Party' at Viking Villa in Palm Springs wearing an asymmetrical tartan skirt.

    Stella Maxwell, Riccardo Tisci and Irina Shayk:

    The fashion set, including supermodels Stella Maxwell and Irina Shayk as well as Burberry's former creative director Riccardo Tisci were also spotted at Coachella's Marc Jacobs & i-D 'Pre-Party.'

    Adut Akech:

    Supermodel Adut Akech opted for a floral maxi dress and sandals to attend Coachella's Marc Jacobs & i-D 'Pre-Party.' 

    Ebony Riley:

    Model and singer-songwriter Ebony Riley attended the Interscope Coachella Party wearing yellow trousers and a black crop top. 

    Inanna Sarkis:

    Social media star Inanna Sarkis opted for the Y2K aesthetic to attend Coachella this year, she donned a Gen Z approved butterfly embellished crop top and white cargo trousers and silver platform boots.

    Billie Eilish:

    Billie Eilish performed with Labrinth at the Mojave Tent in an oversized black parka and black baseball cap.

    Rosé:

    Rosé of Blackpink performed at the Coachella Stage in a black co-ord.

    Rosalía:

    Rosalía performed at the Coachella Stage in a futuristic mask.

    Jisoo:

    Jisoo of Blackpink performed at the Coachella Stage in a red anthurium covered dress.

    Jennie:

    Jennie of Blackpink performed at the Coachella Stage in a crystal embellished crop top, black hot pants and leather thigh-high boots.

    Angele:

    Belgian singer, and Hello! Fashion’s June 2022 cover girl Angele performed on April 14 wearing a sparkly Paris Hilton-approved co-ord and frilly socks and white trainers.

     Shanina Shaik:

     Shanina Shaik wears a black mini dress and black cowboy boots.

    Alessandra Ambrosio:

    Coachella style queen Alessandra Ambrosio shows off her signature 1970s inspired festival style in a crochet crop top and tan flares.

    Sara Sampaio:

    Sara Sampaio attends the CELSIUS Oasis Vibe House at Coachella in lime green mini shorts and a Y2K butterfly top.

    Josephine Skriver and Jasmine Tookes:

    Supermodels Josephine Skriver and Jasmine Tookes show off their individual style at Coachella.

    Madison Beer:

    Singer Madison Beer wears black shorts and a black crochet bralet.

    Delilah Belle Hamlin:

    Delilah Belle Hamlin makes a case for the visible thong at Coachella this year.

    Teyana Taylor:

    Teyana Taylor stopped by the Heineken House in a leather folk-inspired jacket, gold face tattoos and olive coloured parachute trousers.

    Kathryn Newton:

    Big Little Lies star Kathryn Newton takes style cues from the Brit pack in a ditzy floral print dress and skinny scarf.

    Becky G:

    American singer and actress Rebbeca Marie Gomez, known professionally as Becky G performs onstage in a vivid shade of royal blue and matching cowboy hat.
