    Understated but iconic, this dress watch dates back to the 1990s but has been fitted with fresh black leather straps. It's the perfect discreet finisher to everyday looks.

    Futuristic Steel

    Futuristic Steel

    Credit: Vestiaire Collective

    A 2000's design, this watch has a spacey feel to it with its futuristic black watch face. It's crafted from polished stainless steel and fastens with a silver buckle at the back.

    Modern Bamboo

    Modern Bamboo

    Credit: Vestiaire Collective

    With a stainless-steel face and matte black strap, this polished watch offers a pared-back twist on the house's Bamboo signature.

    Gucci Gold

    Gucci Gold

    Credit: 1st Dibs

    Designed some time in the noughties – Gucci's most sexed-up era –this gilded metal watch is the ultimate statement. It's chunky, but not overly so, and will look especially striking against pillarbox-red nails.

    1970s Nostalgia

    1970s Nostalgia

    Credit: 1st Dibs

    A rare find from the 1970s, this 18kt yellow-gold plated watch is fitted with tan leather straps and finished with Roman numerals on the face. Consider it proof that classic designs never go out of style.

    Noughties Revival

    Noughties Revival

    Credit: Farfetch

    For major impact, turn to Tom Ford's high-octane Gucci tenure. This watch takes a more-is-more approach to the house's Horsebit motif, weaving it around a slick, square watch face.

    Chain Link

    Chain Link

    Credit: Vestiaire Collective

    This retro Gucci watch offer a bold flash of gold without feeling too bulky on the wrist. It's striking yet slimline, and features Roman numerals running around the outside of the face.

    Web Stripes

    Web Stripes

    Credit: Vestiaire Collective

    The slim gold bracelet strap of this watch keeps all focus on the red and green Web stripes that frame the face: an iconic Gucci signifier.

    Signature Stripes

    Signature Stripes

    Credit: 1st Dibs

    Instantly recognisable as a Gucci design with the signature stripes, this model features gold-plated pin buckles and a brown snakeskin leather strap.
