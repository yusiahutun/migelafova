    A fashion staple that will never go out of style, a satin shirt is a must-have for any wardrobe. They're easy to wear, they can be dressed up or down, and the sleek, elegant design makes them so flattering for any occasion.

    Whether you're looking for a smart colour block piece to wear to work or a chic oversized fit for an effortless daywear style, scroll the edit of the best satin shirts to shop for spring.

    Best satin shirts for women

    • Buttoned Satin Shirt

      Mango's satin shirt comes in the most gorgeous aqua green shade, and it looks far more expensive than it is.

    • Long Sleeve Satin Shirt

      Marks & Spencer has a great selection of satin pieces right now, and this sleek ivory shirt will look amazing paired with all trouser styles. 

    • Fitted Satin Shirt

      H&M's fitted satin shirt is the ultimate staple that can be reached for all year round. We recommend teaming it with a pair of leather trousers for a sophisticated workwear style, or opt for a fitted midi skirt and heels to glam up the timeless look.

    • Hanson Satin Shirt

      We can't get enough of Reformation's short sleeve satin shirt. The relaxed, boxy fit is so chic - and it's just perfect for spring styling.

    • Frame Stretch-Silk Satin Skirt

      Looking for a satin shirt to reach for whenever you want to look perfectly put together? This Frame stretch-silk style is the investment piece you need.

    • Satin Long Sleeve Shirt

      River Island's satin style with an oversized collar is so classy, and we love it teamed with the matching trousers.

    • Stripe Satin Shirt

      Striped shirts are everywhere right now, and New Look's black and white number adds that extra something to a classic piece.

    • Blouson Sleeve Satin Shirt

      Boden's satin shirt is so feminine with its vintage-style fit and lightly puffed sleeves.

    • Woven Satin Shirt

      Karen Millen knows how to nail sophisticated styles, and this relaxed satin shirt is ideal for an elevated evening look.

    • JYD Satin Shirt

      If you're looking to inject some colour into your spring wardrobe, JDY's pink satin shirt is the one.

    • Gradient Satin Shirt

      Stylish wrap design? Flattering tie back? Unique gradient print? This grey satin shirt has it all.
