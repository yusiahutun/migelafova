    Royal Style Watch: from Queen Camilla's tribute to the late Queen to Princess Charlene's disco fever

    As we near the end of March, we look back at what a month of royal style it has shaped up to be. Meghan Markle kept us on our toes all month with several looks from her visit to Texas with Prince Harry that ranged from chic satin to casual denim.

    24 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Queen Letizia and Princess Anne reign supreme among the royal style set this month

      As we near the end of March, we look back at what a month of royal style it has shaped up to be. 

      Meghan Markle kept us on our toes all month with several looks from her visit to Texas with Prince Harry that ranged from chic satin to casual denim.

      Then there was Cheltenham Festival. Nearly every senior British royal was in attendance with Zara Tindall leading the way with strong outfits with fabulous fascinators on the daily.

      As we draw the month to a close, our attention falls to European royal ladies including Queen Mary of Denmark and Queen Letizia of Spain who have proved their style credentials with stunning cinched looks that prove smart tailoring never goes out of style. Monaco's Princess Charlene also led the way at the  Bal de la Rose 2024 where she served an unforgettable sequinned look.

      We also can't forget the array of royal brooches from the final week of the month that have featured unexpected insects.

      Who's been on our Royal Style Watch this March?

      Queen Camilla

      Queen Camilla was spotted at Worcester Cathedral this week looking supremely elegant in a white fitted coat dress by Fiona Clare with boxy shoulders.

      She paired the look with a fur-adorned hat by Lock & Co, tan suede knee-high boots and a stunning finishing touch - matching clip floral brooches which were gifted to the late Queen Elizabeth by her parents in the 1940s.
