    Prominent Entertainment Attorney Lisa Lester Named Partner At Rothenberg, Mohr &amp; Binder LLP

    20 Jul 2024, 00:00

    LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – Rothenberg, Mohr & Binder LLP, a prominent music and entertainment law firm operating in Los Angeles, New York, and Miami, announced the elevation of Lisa Lester to the position of Partner within the organization.

    Known in the industry as a full-service law entertainment law firm, Rothenberg, Mohr & Binder LLP caters to a wide array of clients, ranging from artists and songwriters to music companies and startups. Among its notable clientele are Gunna, Jazmine Sullivan, Marshmello, and Logic, among others

    Lester, who is based in Los Angeles, first joined Rothenberg, Mohr & Binder LLP in March 2020 and works across the firm’s roster, including Marshmello, Top Dawg Entertainment, Jake Wesley Rogers, LU KALA and Big Time Rush.

    Lester also represents a diverse list of talent including Grammy and Latin Grammy Award-winning mixer Rob Kinelski and producer Noah Goldstein, DOMi & JD BECK, BUNT, among other songwriters, producers, music executives and artists.

    “We are excited to elevate Lisa to partner at the firm. Lisa’s dedication to clients and work on behalf of the firm is second to none – and this promotion is well deserved. Her prior years of experience across the industry and high level work here at the firm since 2020 have given her unique insight and expertise that few others share. We are honored to welcome Lisa to our group of amazing partners and associates, and we look forward to continued growth together here at the firm,” stated the firm’s Founding Partners Paul Rothenberg, Jeremy Mohr and Josh Binder in a joint statement.

    “I’m absolutely thrilled and deeply honored to become a partner at Rothenberg, Mohr & Binder, LLP. My commitment to advocating for our exceptional roster of clients remains unwavering, and I am grateful for the opportunity to further our firm’s reputation and catalyze its continued growth,” Lester added.
