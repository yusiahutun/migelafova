    Meaning of Do It To Em by Future

    11 Aug 2024, 00:00

    In Future's song "Do It To Em," the overarching theme of the track revolves around success, confidence, and the extravagant lifestyle that comes with it. The lyrics paint a vivid picture of Future's opulent lifestyle and his unapologetic approach to his accomplishments. Each line showcases his dominance and asserts his place at the top of the game. While the song may seem straightforward at first glance, upon closer examination, one can uncover deeper and unexpected themes that add layers of complexity to the track.One standout lyric from the song is, "Put the VVS in my necklace had to do it to em." The mention of VVS, which refers to diamond clarity, signifies Future's commitment to luxury and his desire to stand out from the crowd. The theme that emerges from this line is the need for individuals to establish their unique identity and express themselves unapologetically. It suggests that to make a lasting impact, one must go above and beyond what is expected of them.Another critical lyric is, "Had to with purple label urban wear was boring." This line highlights Future's inclination for exclusivity and his refusal to conform to the norm. It suggests the theme of constantly pushing boundaries and seeking out individuality in personal style and taste. Future's decision to break away from what is considered the norm in fashion reinforces the idea that success is not just about material wealth but also about forging a distinct and memorable path."Gone park the old school, it's time to ride foreign." This line showcases Future's desire for progression and his willingness to leave behind the past for what lies ahead. It reflects the theme of embracing change and constantly seeking out new challenges and experiences. The juxtaposition of the "old school" and the "foreign" represents the transition from the familiar to the unfamiliar, suggesting that growth and success require stepping out of one's comfort zone.These unexpected themes in "Do It To Em" ultimately tie back to the overarching theme of the song â€“ the pursuit of success and the extravagant lifestyle that comes with it. Future's use of vivid imagery and assertive language creates a narrative that encourages listeners to embrace their uniqueness, challenge societal norms, and constantly evolve to achieve greatness.Through these imaginative themes, Future elevates the song beyond the surface-level narrative of success and status. He encourages listeners to reflect on their own journey and emboldens them to break barriers and leave their mark on the world. "Do It To Em" serves as an anthem to inspire individuals to be unapologetically themselves and strive for greatness, not only in material wealth but also in personal growth and self-expression.
