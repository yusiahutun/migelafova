    Who is considered a mumble rapper?

    They are rappers that use mumbling or unclear vocal delivery in their songs. Their content or lyrics revolve around sex, drug, partying, designer clothing, money, and jewellery.

    Who was the first mumble rapper?

    There is no definitive answer to who the first mumble rapper was. Some rappers credited with influencing the mumble rap style include Young Thug, Future, Trippie Redd and Lil Baby.

    Is Kendrick a mumble rapper?

    Kendrick Lamar is not considered a mumble rapper. He is known for his clear and articulate lyrics. He emphasizes his words and delivers them with precision, the opposite of what defines mumble rap.

    Is Travis Scott a mumble rapper?

    No, he is not. Travis Scott is a gifted lyricist and rapper despite his heavy reliance on autotune. He even described himself as not hip-hop because of his style and atmosphere. Travis Scott's musical style includes hip hop, Southern hip hop, trap, psychedelic, and pop rap.

    Was Eminem a mumble rapper?

    No, he wasn't. Eminem is among the most talented lyrists hip hop has ever seen. His fast and rapid rap style is unique and is not mumble rapping. According to Genius, mumble rappers tend to use the "aye" flow, where they add words such as "yeah", "aye", and "uh" to the start or end of their lines.

    Who are the best female mumble rappers?

    Some few female rappers associated with the mumble rap style include Rico Nasty, SoFaygo and Kash Doll.

    Who are considered as the worst mumble rappers?

    According to views from Quora, the worst mumble rappers are 21 Savage, 6ix9ine, A Boogie wit da Hoodie, BlocBoy JB, Chief Keef and Denzel Curry.

    Why is mumble rap hated?

    Its lyrics is perceived to lack clarity. Listeners might find it hard to understand what the rapper is saying. Some feel like mumble rap glorifies materialism and a certain lifestyle.
