    KCB Simba Points is a loyalty program founded by the Kenya Commercial Bank with the aim of enabling their clients to earn points from transacting through deposits, cards, mobile banking, insurance, internet banking, and loans. This program offers KCB customers several redemptions on the earned Simba Points KCB Group. These include an array of online shops, retail outlets, KCB banking services as well as access to more than 450,000 hotels, 900 airlines, and 150,000 car rental organizations.

    You can sign up for KCBBank Simbapoints via several channels including paying a visit to a KCB bank branch or through the KCB App, KCB Mobi Banking, and Internet Banking. After that, you will be required to activate your KCB Simba account to begin earning Simba Points anytime you carry out any of those transactions mentioned earlier. After some time when you will have earned a good number of points, you will now be eligible to redeem them. Additionally, you are also allowed to transfer your Simba Points to other KCB customers.

    KCB Simba points registration

    To participate, you need to register for this program through the channels below:

  • Branch enrolment – If you already have an account with KCB, you will be required to fill out the amendment form. New members will fill in the account opening forms, ticking the loyalty program box.
  • Mobi Bank – You will first dial *522# then enter your Mobi PIN. You will then select My Account, go to Simba Points, then select Enroll. You will then receive an SMS confirming your successful enrollment.

    • You can also use the easy to follow registration steps of the KCB App.

    How to earn Simba points

    The accrual of your KCB Simba Points is always by your registration and usage of the program, your KCB accounts, and any other criteria that will be communicated from time to time. By spending on your debit card, you will earn two points for every Ksh. 1,000 you spend. On the KCB Advantage (Gold/premium), you will gain four points. By spending using your debit card (Pepea and Me Cash), you will also earn two points for a Ksh. 1,000 expenditure. Credit cards such as Classic Gold and Platinum will also earn you two, four, and four points respectively.

    How to check Simba Points balance

    First, dial *522# and enter your Mobi PIN. Select My Account, Simba Points, and then Balance Enquiry. You can also use the KCB online Simba Points Portal. Here, you will key in your KCB login details and password, select My Account, and then Check Account Summary.

    KCB Simba points terms and conditions

    KCB Simba Points program is open to any participant who chooses to register on the program. However, corporate customers or any other class of business customers that will be communicated by KCB will be excluded. Enrollment for the program is free of charge, and a participant will be deemed to have been successfully enrolled once they receive a confirmation SMS from KCB to verify the same.

    Notably, as account holders, customers may fail to qualify to redeem if their accounts have been overdrawn or have zero balance. In such instances, you will forfeit the Simba Points you will have earned for any dormant or inactive account and overdue loans. Moreover, if you don’t pay appraisal fees for your mortgage, you won’t earn any points.

    KCB Simba points redeem options

    You can redeem the Simba Points you have earned for merchandise, KCB products, and services, as well as any other rewards offered by the bank from time to time. The organization usually communicates any information concerning any available rewards and the redemption requirements that apply from time to time. This is done via the press, on the KCB site, or any other means.
