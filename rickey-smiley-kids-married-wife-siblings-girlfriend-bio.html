    Rickey Smiley Bio

    The prank caller and reality TV star is an Alabama-born comedian who was born on August 10, 1968. His real name is Broderick Smiley but he calls himself Rickey and grew up in Birmingham.

    Rickey is African American and attended Alabama State University and Tuskegee University for his education. During his studies at Alabama State University, he was a member of the Omega Psi Phi Phi Fraternity and the Kappa Kappa Psi National Honorary Band Fraternity.

    Rickey’s career in comedy began in 2000 as host of the BET show Comic View. He excelled in his role as host and was promptly signed on for five years. A formal contract with the TV Network enabled him to host other television shows such as The Nashville Network Comic Escape and Uptown Comedy Club. He has also worked on HBO’s legendary comedy show Def Comedy Jam.

    Before Rickey found his niche in prank phone calls he mostly played outrageous fictional characters. He found his place in the comedy world when he started working for a Dallas radio station CBFB in 2004. He was offered the chance to be the host of the morning radio show. One of the outstanding features of the show was the distinctive telephone conversations for which Rickey is known today.

    Together with the information news and the playing of the latest hip-hop music the show quickly became popular among listeners. In 2008 he was offered to show his hit show nationwide and he accepted the offer.

    The show “The Rickey Smiley Morning Show” gained great popularity through prank calls where the comedian disguises his voice to amuse the audience and calls a random number to amuse the listeners. One of the most notable pranks he has performed is “Uncle Melvin” where he calls his uncle and pretends to be a man who is convinced that his girlfriend has left him. He developed the joke further by making sure that the character punctuated each of his remarks with the word “buddy”.

    Due to the popularity of his show especially the prank calls Rickey Smiley has released albums of his prank calls and six albums in total with the album “Rickey Smiley”: Vol 6 is a best-selling comedy album on iTunes. Outside of radio Rickey has appeared in several movies and television shows including his reality TV show Rickey Smiley for Real in 2015 and his filmography lists movies such as Friday after Next (2002) and Baggage Claim (2013).

    In his July 6 YouTube video, Smiley expressed his gratitude to God for safeguarding his daughter.

    Rickey Smiley Education

    Smiley attended Woodlawn High School for his high school education and then proceeded to Alabama State University in Montgomery Alabama where he earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in English in 1992.

    Who are Smiley’s parents?

    Rickey was born to Carolita Smiley Lester but his father tragically passed away when he was only six years old. To ensure Rickey had the support and guidance he needed his mother relied on her extended family to help raise him; as such Rickey spent most of his childhood being cared for by his grandparents.

    His grandmother was his biggest influence in life instilling a strong faith and moral values by bringing him to church regularly attending funerals together and raising him with the teachings of the church. Her unconditional love and guidance have been instrumental in forming who he is today.

After the death of his father, Rickey was raised by his grandparents. Although he is an only child Rickey has expressed on several occasions that much of his early life was fun due to the strong sense of community of the southern culture and the fact that he was surrounded by many family members.
