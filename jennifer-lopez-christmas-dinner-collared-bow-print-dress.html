    Jennifer Lopez looked gorgeous while hosting her Christmas dinner.

    The superstar has shared new pics from her family's Christmas weekend, where she culminated a season of holiday-themed looks with a merry and bright piece. In the snaps, comprised of a selfie in front of a set dinner table and a full shot by a tinsel-adorned tree, the "Let's Get Loud" singer looks festive in a shiny retro-style dress printed with red bows on a turquoise-blue background.

    The knee-length piece featured structured shoulders, a matching belt with a flouncy bow, white cuffs, and a white pointed collar with red poppy appliques. She finished the look with sparkly red platform heels and a pair of large white-and-gold floral earrings. As for her beauty look, the star slicked her hair back and wore a dark red lip.

    "Merry Christmas!!!!" she captioned the post.

    The Maid in Manhattan star has spent the past holiday season enjoying her first year celebrating with her husband Ben Affleck, after the pair tied the knot in two whirlwind ceremonies earlier this year. A source previously told Entertainment Tonight that the pair were planning to go "all out" for their first Christmas as a married couple, saying that Lopez "wants this year to be even more special than usual and to make new traditions of their own, both as a family and with Ben."

    In addition to the blended family's private celebrations on Christmas weekend, the newlyweds also threw a lavish holiday bash at their Hollywood home last weekend, welcoming a star-studded guest list that included Kim Kardashian, Doja Cat, Billie Eilish, and Jane Fonda.

    Partygoers shared now-deleted photos and videos from the party on social media, which showed the Afflecks' stunning festive decorations, including a tent-like light display, a Christmas tree, and a piano, where the couple and their guests gave intimate musical performances.
