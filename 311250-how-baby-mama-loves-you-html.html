Some guys end up living blindly without knowing that the scales have already tipped and its time to walk. There are many ways on how to tell if your baby mama still loves you or her attention has shifted elsewhere for good, but you need to be keen.

    A baby mama is a term coined to refer to the mother of your child outside marriage. It is not rocket science to tell if one party in the relationship is no longer interested. What is challenging is understanding the accuracy of these signs and whether they are reflective of what the other party is feeling.     How to tell if your baby mama still loves you

    The most asked question is whether or not the baby changes anything in the relationship, and the answer is a definite "Yes." However, some changes arise, especially on the side of the lady. It gets complicated when the previous gestures of love or affection are replaced by sideshows and conflict. To ascertain if your baby mama still wants you, here are some signs to watch out for:

    1. She starts calling you more often

    The essence of having a relationship in the first place is to have healthy communication and companionship. When the conversation gets dim after some time, then there is a problem and trouble is brewing. However, when your baby mama calls to check on you more often than before, that is a sign that she still wants you.

    2. Starts acknowledging you as her baby daddy

    Some signs are too obvious to associate with sincere affection. However, the reality is that appreciating him and admitting that "he is my baby daddy" among others means a bid deal. When she respects you and appreciates your effort, the love candle gets livelier and shines brighter.

    3. She starts asking for more time with you

    If she asks you to stay around a little bit longer, then it is evident that your child's mother still loves you. Distance is the biggest enemy when in a fight, and has been translated as the widening of a wedge. However, you can join the dots when she wants you around more than usual and respond to her signals effectively.

    4. She starts being possessive

    When your baby mama starts gaining more interests, the first behaviour change is that she will begin being possessive and want you to only sing her tune. If you guys had fought for some time to the extent of pulling the trigger, then it's time to get back with your baby mama. At times she may not be able to say it, but this is a sign that she wants and loves you.

    5. When she starts to leave "I love you" messages

    When she starts confessing the worlds of love in texts through your WhatsApp and other chat platforms, there is no other proof to wait for. Even if the last time you guys shared the sweet words, "I Love You" was a long time ago, you ought to understand that she is confessing her affection when you notice a change in her communication.

    Signs of a jealous baby mama

    In other instances, some actions show your baby mama is crazy about you to the point of developing jealousy. While most people will misinterpret this as a clingy and too demanding, it means she wants you for herself and herself alone. Here are signs that will tell you she is jealous:

    1. Loses her cool whenever she sees you texting

    Well, ladies can be too possessive of what they believe is theirs. She will sense danger whenever she feels that your love for her is exchanging hands with another woman. Sometimes, these feelings are not founded on anything tangible, but the fact that her instincts sense danger, she will fight back.

    2. She starts to get annoyed by some of your behaviours

    Some of the behaviors she will feel uncomfortable about include coming it the house late or disliking some of the posts on your social media. Well, it is not that she doesn't like you being in your best, but she can't take the fact that someone else may spot you outside there.

    3. Starts questioning about some of your friends and their intentions

    The extent of jealousy for baby mamas is farfetched. Even though she knows your friends from long ago comprising of both gents and ladies, these people start becoming a bother to her if they are taking any additional attention that could be channelled to her. Do not be surprised when she asks you about your whereabouts with your friends. It only means she is jealous.

    4. Starts calling you irregularly

    We mentioned that her calling you is a sign that she wants you back. However, she may flip to the extreme side of things and start making calls for reasons not justifiable. For instance, she may call you on short intervals while at work or away on official duty to confirm that you are there and not some other place with someone else's.

    After breaking up, most couples tend to hang around for some time while others opt to move on. However, you should be able to read signs or pointers that indicate your baby mama may not just be over you yet and wants the father of her child back. Here are some signs to watch out for:

    1. She is more comfortable when around you

    You will notice a sudden change in the way she behaves around you or when you visit. Unlike when it was tensed, and she looked stiff after the breakup, this time she will even suggest things you wouldn't envision between the two of you ever again.

If you are interested, this is the time you can get your baby mama to fall in love with you all over again.

    3. She still refers to herself as part of you

    After breaking up, you guys could have parted ways angry and unhappy. However, do not be surprised when you notice some unexpected behaviours from your baby mama. She may start suggesting ideas like going to meet your family and friends. For instance, if she asks to accompany you to your sister's graduation or a family event, then it is clear she still wants you back.

    5. She suddenly starts being your closest friend

    It is a rare thing for someone who once hated you for ruining her life to start getting closer and getting more concerned about your affairs.     6. Her friends become more indulged and concerned about you
