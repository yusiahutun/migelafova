    Abe Forman-Greenwald Net Worth

    Abe Forman-Greenwald garnered a net worth of under $2 million by 2021.

    Talking about his work, Abe has been a producer since the beginning of the 2000s. His first known job was with 20th Century Fox where he produced, filmed, and edited DVD bonus materials for the film Little Manhattan (2005). He then went on to work as an editor for Aardman Animations, Creature Comforts, and The Simpsons Ride Pre-Show for 2 years straight.

    In April 2008, Abe joined Actual Reality Pictures as a segment producer, shooting and interviewing families for TLC’s “Hope For Your Home” — a home makeover show related to the mortgage crisis.

    The same year, he also worked on a documentary series about Iraq and Afghanistan veterans for the online distribution and PBS affiliates, In Their Boots.

    In Oct 2010, Abe produced and directed short content for a multi-media production company, The Man’s Guide To Love, for almost 2 years. Then he went on to join The Huggington Post as a studio producer of 40 minutes of live broadcast each weekday.

    After over a year of his time on The Huggington Post, Abe was then hired by BuzzFeed Motion Pictures where he directed and produced BuzzFeed’s first long-form documentary “Brother Orange”

    Finally, in September 2016, Abe got his dream job working in Netflix hit animate series Big Mouth. He was working there as of 2021.
