    Tikal is an anthropomorphic echidna spirit and the daughter of Chief Pachacamac of the Knuckles Clan. She had disappeared 3,000 years ago and came to Sonic Adventure as a non-playable character.

    The young Rabbit is often seen with a Chao called Cheese. Cream the Rabbit is cultured, polite, and kind and often portrays childlike habits. Her large ears are some of her most notable features, not just because of their size but also because she uses them to fly by flapping.
