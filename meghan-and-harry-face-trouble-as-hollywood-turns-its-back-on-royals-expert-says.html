    Meghan and Harry face 'trouble as Hollywood turns its back' on royals, expert says

    Outkick host Tomi Lahren claims Prince Harry and Meghan Markles journey in Hollywood is not going as well as they may have expected. The royals are facing trouble as they suffer not only from wokeism but narcissism and laziness, the host alleges in a recent TV interview.

    31 Mar 2024, 00:00

    Outkick host Tomi Lahren claims Prince Harry and Meghan Markle’s journey in Hollywood is not going as well as they may have expected. The royals are facing ‘trouble’ as they suffer not “only from wokeism but narcissism and laziness,” the host alleges in a recent TV interview.

    Since Meghan and her husband Prince Harry have moved to California, life hasn’t always been easier for the duo. The couple’s bombshell interview with Oprah Winfrey and Netflix series caused a stir. While the Duke and Duchess of Sussex highlighted ‘their side’ of the story, many believe it caused further distance with the Firm.

    Prince Harry and Meghan “think highly of themselves”

    Royal commentator Tomi Lahren is unapologetic about her opinions on Prince Harry and Meghan Markle.

    She claims to Sky News Australia: “Prince Harry and Meghan think highly of themselves.” She then added the royal couple does not actually want to put in the effort it takes to be working members of The Firm.

    “They also don’t want to work very hard beyond their royal titles which they continually c**p on. So they try to have it both ways.” The expert then said Hollywood is “turning on these two.”

    Recently, sources close to the royal couple had claimed Harry and Meghan’s dream of making it big in Hollywood is intact, but Tomi thinks their days of basking in glory may come to an end.

    She offered: “When they moved to the United States, they really were put on a silver platter like we would be so happy to have them. (But now it’s like) I will tell you all, you can gladly have them back.”

    The ‘Hollywood’ dream of Prince Harry and Meghan Markle

    Prince Harry and Meghan are coming back with new content soon, the former TV star teased at Variety’s Power of Women event in November.

    “We have so many exciting things on the slate. I can’t wait until we can announce them, but I’m just really proud of what we’re creating,” the Suits star had said. “My husband is loving it, too. It’s really fun.”

    Echoing the Duchess of Sussex, an insider also revealed what’s next for the royal pair.

    “They’re coming up with more content and they’re very excited about what the future holds. It’s been a learning curve and their dream of making it big in Hollywood is still alive and well,” the source dished out.

    Moreover, Harry and Meghan are also seen cozying up with billionaires at Katy Perry’s concert, which could be a major change for their careers. While not much is known about their future goals, reports say Meghan is planning her big comeback with the motto of giving back to the community.

    The source informed: “Meghan has a new team around her, and they’ve been strategizing about what mediums will have the most impact.” They added that Meghan is interested in “giving back” through her work and “philanthropy.”
