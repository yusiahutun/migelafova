    When you think of the Dutton ranch, you likely think of dusty cowboy boots, but it's time to think again. Yellowstone and HEYDUDE are partnering up for the collab we didn't know we needed. The comfortable shoe brand has teamed up with Paramount for a cozy shoe inspired by the most-watched show on TV.

    HEYDUDE launched the collection exclusively on their website on November 7. The put a Western twist on their classic Wendy and Wally silhouettes with a look inspired by fan-favorite couple Beth Dutton and Rip Wheeler. Rest assured, they offer the same slip-on comfort you expect from HEYDUDE shoes.

    HEYDUDE

    The men's Wally shoe is made from black canvas with a Yellowstone printed insole and flexible outsole. The Wendy shoe for women features a stonewash canvas upper with leopard-print lining, a faux shearling collar, and gold beads on the laces. Both are embroidered with the trademark Yellowstone logo. The men's shoe comes in sizes 6 to 15, while the women's shoe ranges from 5 to 12. But are priced at $74.99.

    If you're looking for a gift for the Yellowstone fan in your life, this might be the perfect choice. Or buy a pair for yourself—they're a super relaxing choice to wear when you kick up your feet and re-watch the series before it returns next year.
