    Hailey Bieber Updates the Little Black Dress via an Unexpected Neckline

    Hailey Bieber and little black dresses are a match made in heaven. The closet staple is currently having a moment in Hailey's wardrobe, who has been spotted in different iterations of the classic dress style over the past month.

    15 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Hailey Bieber and little black dresses are a match made in heaven. The closet staple is currently having a moment in Hailey's wardrobe, who has been spotted in different iterations of the classic dress style over the past month.

    During a recent outing to Los Angeles celeb hotspot Giorgio Baldi with husband Justin Bieber, the Rhode founder once again relied on the LBD—this time with a twist.

    The dress featured the requisite short hemline, but upended the traditional expectations of the LBD by including a structured halter that hung around the model's neck like a thick collar necklace. At the front, the neckline of the dress also curved downwards, sloping into a low-back silhouette. Hailey finished the look off with black suede pumps, ultra-sheer tights from Calzedonia, a black patent leather shoulder bag from Saint Laurent, and tiny gold hoop earrings.

    Meanwhile, Justin went for a more laidback look. The "Peaches" singer wore a gray crewneck sweater, baggy white trousers, a black leather aviator jacket with dark brown fur trim on the collar, white slip-on sandals, a blue-and-white baseball cap, and black sunglasses.

    BACKGRID

    The last time Hailey wore a LBD was earlier this month during a night out in New York City. Then, the model looked fabulous in a strapless mini with a bedazzled golden buckle at the bust.

    Prior to that appearance, Hailey shared a look at another black dress from her closet via Instagram, in which she showed off an elegant suede piece by Ferragamo, which included long sleeves, a turtleneck, and a stunning open back.
