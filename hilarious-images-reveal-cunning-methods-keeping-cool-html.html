    Hilarious images reveal cunning methods for keeping cool

    Millions in Britain and America are struggling to keep their cool amid ferocious heatwaves. But help is at hand. These photos reveal some extremely cunning ways to ward off extreme temperatures. And they're cheap, too.

    27 May 2024, 00:00

    Millions in Britain and America are struggling to keep their cool amid ferocious heatwaves.

    But help is at hand.

    These photos reveal some extremely cunning ways to ward off extreme temperatures. And they're cheap, too.

    One man from the UK strapped a desk fan to his head in a desperate bid for a breeze, while another, in Asia, surrounded himself with more than 13 room fans before comfortably reclining in a deckchair.

    Other inventive souls set up makeshift offices in their fridges, with cold beverages within easy reach. But for one, only a wheelie bin filled with ice would seemingly suffice.

    Scroll down for MailOnline Travel's round-up of zany cooling off methods.

    Cold water bottle: A man in Thailand took a large block of ice to bed with him in a bid to keep cool. Extreme, but seemingly effective

    Chilling out: A British resident had a cunning idea to turn his wheeling dustbin into an ice plunge pool. He gave the refreshing soak a big thumbs up

    Soaking it all in: This man has gone for a bowls-of-water-fan combo to battle the heat

    A man was snapped sleeping in a store refrigerator as temperatures climbed (left) while another had a similar idea, as he shifted his workspace to the kitchen unit (right)
