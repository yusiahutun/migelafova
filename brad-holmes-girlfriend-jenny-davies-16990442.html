    01 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Jenny Davies – the long-suffering girlfriend of online prankster Brad Holmes – has gained a reputation for her perceived lack of knowledge.

    She went viral last year after for claiming to be faster than Usain Bolt, as well as offering some ridiculous reasons to vote for Brexit.

    But her latest moment of madness has gone one step further – when she attempted to show how she was a better diver than British Olympian Daley.

    In a video posted to Facebook yesterday (August 2), Jenny is seen rowing with her partner over her ability to dive.

    “I have [mastered diving] Brad in one day,” she says.

    He fires back: “One day is not enough – Tom Daley took years to master diving and he's still not winning everything.”

    Jenny responds by claiming “they’re s***” and that she could “win the Olympics with my diving in one day”.

    Brad continues to remonstrate the ridiculous claims so his girlfriend walks over to a nearby swimming pool to prove her point.

    But instead of diving majestically into the water, she causes an almighty splash with a painful-looking belly flop.

    The YouTube star is given a second chance to prove her ability, but it ends even worse when she takes a run up.

    Brad bursts out in hysterics at the farcical scene, before Jenny returns to the sun–bed, saying: “Told ya – that was sick mate.”
