    Gotham//Getty Images

    Chopra wore a feathered Marchesa gown to attend her bridal shower at Tiffany's Blue Box Café in New York. She later defended her choice of dress, saying: “Georgina’s a friend of mine - and it’s not her fault. I don’t think it is right to take it out on a self-made woman what somebody in her life did. That’s the wrong attitude. That was a beautiful gown and deserved to be worn by a bride-to-be. It made me feel like a princess. It was the right choice.”
