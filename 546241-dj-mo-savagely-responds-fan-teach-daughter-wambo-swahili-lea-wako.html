    DJ Mo Savagely Responds to Fan Asking Him to Teach Daughter Wambo Kiswahili: &quot;Lea Wako&quot;

    Pastor Size 8's husband, DJ Mo, has silenced an online fan who tried to offer him advice concerning her daughter Ladasha Wambo. The fan was reacting to a video shared by Mo as he and his wife had taken their children to an exquisite toy store.

    • The online fan advised the father of two to do so so that his lovely daughter Wambo could navigate the streets of Nairobi
    • Mo did not take his advice lightly as he responded to him in the comments, urging him to raise his children

    Pastor Size 8's husband, DJ Mo, has silenced an online fan who tried to offer him advice concerning her daughter Ladasha Wambo.

    Which toy did DJ Mo's daughter pick?

    The fan was reacting to a video shared by Mo as he and his wife had taken their children to an exquisite toy store.

    They shopped for toys and started with Wambo's before proceeding to their son, Sammy Muraya Junior's.

    "Family time - It is the brain," read the caption of the family video, which garnered many comments and reactions.

    Wambo picked up a toy that looked cylindrical and had a doll inside. She revealed that it was called a Barbie Color Change.

    DJ Mo's daughter explains Barbie Color Change

    In her unique English accent, she explained it was simply a toy that changed colours when immersed in water.

    Her accent, which always mesmerizes Kenyans, was the main topic in the comment section, except for one.

    One fan advised Mo to teach his cute daughter Kiswahili, but the father of two did not take his suggestion well.

    "Lea wako (Raise yours)," Mo responded to the fan who had asked him to teach her daughter Wambo Kiswahili to "navigate the streets of Nairobi".
