    Ah, spring. It’s the season of transition, and while it’s great in many ways, when it comes to the weather, it can be more frustrating than anything. From brisk, rainy mornings to much warmer afternoons, you truly never know what you’re going to get. This makes finding the best spring jacket for women the ultimate necessity. So what makes a great spring jacket, you ask? Well, it’s got to be lightweight, it’s got to be versatile, and most importantly, it’s got to be flattering. Luckily, there are three classic styles that never fail to fulfill these requirements.  

    The Best Slimming Spring Jacket Styles 

    When shopping a spring jacket, one of the most important factors to consider is fit. You want to make sure your jacket leaves enough room for layers to keep you warm on cooler days, but not so much that you’re swimming in a sea of fabric. You’ll also want to make sure the jacket is structured in a way that’s flattering on your body, leaving you feeling confident every time you step out the door. So what kind of jacket should you be looking for? Here are the three best slimming spring jacket styles every woman should have in her closet this season. 

    1. Blazer. A staple in many women’s closets, a good, solid blazer is a must have for all spring wardrobes. Drawing the eye vertically down past trouble spots, a solid-colored blazer is a stylish piece of outerwear that’ll help trim your torso and streamline your silhouette. 

    FIRST TIP: Nix the bold buttons. We all love a good emblazoned military-style blazer, but shiny oversized buttons draw attention to the belly, magnifying areas we may not be the most comfortable accentuating. 

    2. Trench Coat. When you hear the word spring jacket, chances are visions of trench coats dance in your head. Ideal for keeping warm, dry, and stylish, this figure-flattering style is a go-to for a reason. It’s structured shape and cinching belt instantly defines the waist and compliments your curves in the best way. 

    FIRST TIP: Choose an A-line trench coat. Large lapels, big pockets, and a loose waist can cause boxiness, overwhelming your chest and highlighting your curves in the wrong way. With an A-line cut, you can put your worries behind you and strut your stuff with confidence. 

    3. Anorak. For many women, it’s not spring until they whip out their trust utility jacket, also known as an anorak. Coming in a variety of lengths, these jackets are the perfect lightweight jacket and is usually loaded with pockets. For the most slimming look go for a tailored, hip-length anorak. This style helps you look long, lean, and pulls the eye up and in for a slim, statuesque appearance. 

    FIRST TIP: Always opt for the crop. While they might provide a bit more coverage, roomy, thigh-skimming anoraks cut the body awkwardly, creating a short wide look. 

    The Best Spring Jackets for Women

    Ready to find your perfect blazer? Have a trench coat in need of an update? Dying to add an anorak to your closet? Keep scrolling for FIRST’S picks for the best spring jackets for women that are simple, slimming, and stylish.
