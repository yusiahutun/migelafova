    Is it just us, or does it seem like Easter snuck up on us this year? Ready or not, Easter is here, and if you haven't started planning your Easter menu, the time is now! If the thought of cooking and cleaning and grocery shopping stresses you out, load up the family and head to one of the restaurants open on Easter near you. Save your energy for Easter egg hunts, Instagram-worthy photo ops, and lots of time spent on family traditions. Plus, you won't have to fight all of the last-minute crowds at the grocery stores (unless, of course, you need to pick up some chocolate Easter bunnies).

    Depending on where you live, local restaurants may be offering special menus on April 9. Call ahead to see if your favorite restaurant is offering an Easter dinner or brunch, or try one of these family-friendly nationwide standbys. These restaurants will do the work for you this year while you relax and enjoy the day.

    We recommend calling ahead to check the locations to determine the best way to order and to find out their hours of operation, as they can vary by location. Many restaurants offer reservations or call-ahead seating on Easter, so be sure to plan ahead, especially if you have a larger group. Be sure to check out our list of stores open on Easter in case you want to make a stop while you're out.
