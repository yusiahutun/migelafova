    Jessica Nigri is an American model, cosplay enthusiast, YouTuber, voice actress, and fan convention interview correspondent. She was born on August 5, 1989, in Reno, Nevada, the United States whereas grew up in Christchurch, New Zealand, her mother’s hometown, and carries dual New Zealand-American citizenship. Jessica spent her early adulthood in Phoenix, Arizona, United States. She is a Christian and born to Cory and Jacqueline Nigri.

    Jessica Nigri Career

    Jessica kicked off things in her career over a viral video in 2009, she dressed up as ‘Sexy Pikachu’ in Comic-Con in San Diego, which went viral overnight. This opened up the door to multiple opportunities for Jessica and landed the role of Anya Stroud for Microsoft and GameStop’s Gears of War 3 promotion. In 2011, she played cosplay girl in the music video Chalkskin: T ‘n A and also played Sonya Blade in the music video Nigri Please! in 2012. Throughout her career, she has cosplayed many other characters from different anime and video games such as Rikku from Final Fantasy X-2 and Yoko Littner from Gurren Lagann.

    Jessica won IGN’s contest for a model to portray Juliet Starling. She was later on hired as a spokesmodel by Warner Bros. Games. She has also been in commercials, in 2012, in Zom-Be-Gone, Buzzzzzz Kill as Juliet Starling, and so on. Furthermore, she also signed up with various productions to promote or represent several other comics and video games such as Elsword. In 2013, Suda51, who had hired her to portray Juliet Starling, again hired her to portray Vivienne Squall for the new video game Killer Is Dead.

    Widespread fame

    She has also cosplayed the character Connor Kenway from Assassin’s Creed III and the female version of Captain Edward Kenway, the protagonist of the video game – Assassin’s Creed IV: Black Flag. Jessica’s career as a voice artist started in 2013. In 2013, she signed up for the voice of Cinder Fall for RWBY and is still active with the part. In 2014, she voiced Simmons’ Lieutenant in Red vs. Blue. In Super Sonico: The Animation, she voiced Sonico. She signed up again as Cinder Fall for RWBY Chibi in 2016, which is still on-air.

    Jessica was also a spokesperson for Amazing Arizona Comic Con in 2012, and she was invited to attend many conventions including Anime Expo, Anime Revolution, AVCon, Montreal Comiccon, Anime South, Ottawa Pop Expo as a guest of honor. Apart from cosplay work, she has tried her luck as an interviewer and worked with various media outlets such as GameZone, RUGGED TV, and Comic Book Therapy. Due to her reputation and fame, she has been featured on various YouTube shows which include KassemG and The Philip DeFranco Show. She also started her own channel on YouTube and has 1.2 million followers. In 2018, she starred herself in the documentary Becoming Jessica Nigri.
