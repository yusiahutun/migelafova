    What is Evil Dead Rise?

    Evil Dead Rise is a 2023 horror film written and directed by Lee Cronin. It is the fifth installment in the Evil Dead franchise, which began with the 1981 cult classic The Evil Dead by Sam Raimi. The film stars Lily Sullivan and Alyssa Sutherland as two sisters who have to fight against demonic forces that possess their family members in a Los Angeles apartment building. The film is a standalone entry that does not follow the continuity of the previous films or the TV series Ash vs Evil Dead. The film is produced by Raimi, Rob Tapert, and Bruce Campbell, who also makes a cameo appearance as the voice of the Necronomicon Ex-Mortis, the evil book that unleashes the deadites.

    Why Do People Expect an End Credit Scene?

    End credit scenes, also known as post-credit scenes or stingers, are short clips that play after the main credits of a film. They are often used to tease future sequels, spin-offs, or crossovers, or to provide some extra humor or closure for the audience. End credit scenes have become very popular in recent years, especially in superhero and horror films, as they generate hype and speculation among fans.

    The Evil Dead franchise has a history of using end credit scenes, although not consistently. The first film, The Evil Dead, ended with a cliffhanger that showed the protagonist Ash (Campbell) being attacked by a demon. The second film, Evil Dead II, had a post-credit scene that showed Ash being transported to the Middle Ages, setting up the third film, Army of Darkness. The third film had two alternate endings, one of which was shown as a post-credit scene in some versions. The ending showed Ash oversleeping in a cave and waking up in a post-apocalyptic world. The 2013 reboot, Evil Dead, had a post-credit scene that featured a cameo by Campbell as Ash, who uttered his signature catchphrase “Groovy”. The TV series, Ash vs Evil Dead, also had a post-credit scene in its final episode, that showed Ash waking up in a futuristic world.

    Does Evil Dead Rise Have an End Credit Scene?

    The answer is no. Evil Dead Rise does not have an end credit scene. However, there is a brief sound effect after the credits have ended, that resembles the roar of a demon. This could be interpreted as a hint that the evil is not completely defeated, or as a homage to the original film’s ending. Either way, it does not provide any clear indication of what might happen next in the franchise.

    Therefore, you do not need to wait until the end of the credits to see if there is an extra scene. You can leave the theater as soon as the credits start rolling, or stay and enjoy the music and the names of the people who worked on the film. However, if you are a fan of the franchise, you might want to keep an eye on the news and the social media, as there might be announcements or rumors about the future of Evil Dead. According to Collider, Cronin has expressed his interest in making a sequel, and Campbell has hinted that there might be more spin-offs or crossovers in the works. So, even if there is no end credit scene in Evil Dead Rise, there might be more Evil Dead to come.
