    Bradley Walsh simply couldn't believe his ears when a contestant on The Chase revealed their occupation.

    Most would agree that presenting the ITV quiz show is a pretty sweet gig, but the popular host was blown out of the water when Tracy from Barkshire stepped up for the cashbuilder.

    Making conversation, Bradley asked about what she does for a living, and couldn't have possibly predicted the answer that came back. Somehow, Tracy has landed a job as a 'chocolate taster'.

    Clearly impressed with the contestant, an intrigued Bradley replied: "Chocolate taster. Nice. Who's that for?" Tracy answered: "It's a big international company that own lots of brands across the world. I get to eat chocolate for a living and get paid for it."

    After his initial excitement wore off, Bradley probed deeper, asking what the quizzer does to switch off. With a smile on her face, she laughed: "It's a very stressful job, being a chocolate taster. But my passion is travelling. My partner and I have visited six of the seven continents. We just love it.

    Unsurprisingly, the follow-up question was about her bucket list, with Tracy revealing her dream destinations. She beamed: "I'd like to go to Madagascar and see limas, and I'd love to see a whale shark."

    He chuckled: "Sometimes, you get giant size, or family size, I'm disappointment size. So, you're a professional chocolate taster? Meet a gifted amateur."

    Alas, despite a valiant effort, Tracy failed to cross the finish line, with the formidable general knowledge expert catching her at the last. But before she said her farewells, Bradley had one last favour to ask: "Bring me back a load of Crunchies."
