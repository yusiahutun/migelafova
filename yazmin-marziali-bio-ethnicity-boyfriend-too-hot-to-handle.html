    Yazmin Marziali On Netflix’s Too Hot to Handle

It is worth noting that the Caribbean has not always been the backdrop for Too Hot To Handle, as both Season 2 and Season 3 were filmed in the Turks and Caicos Islands. The first season, on the other hand, took place in a stunning villa in Punta Mita, Mexico.
