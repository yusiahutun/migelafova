    Here is a list of 57 Gadgets you will probably want to try this year;

    1. VII Series Power Outlet

    In 57 Gadgets first is The Austere VII Series power outlet has a sleek design and an equally impressive finish. In addition to a choice of six or eight power outlets, the versatile accessory packs two full USB and three USB-C ports, including a PD-capable one. This means that you can ditch your old adapter and rapidly charge a smartphone by plugging its cable directly into the power outlet.

    2. JBL Quantum 100 Wired Gaming Headphones

    In 57 Gadgets second one is The JBL Quantum 100 wired gaming headphones have an understated design, a lightweight and comfy fit, and a detachable microphone. The latter is great for in-game communications, as well as for making calls while working from home.

    3. Express HD Streaming Media Player

    In 57 Gadgets third one is The Roku Express HD will instantly transform an old TV into a modern home entertainment powerhouse. Easy to set up and use, the affordable gadget can stream Full HD content from all major services, such as Netflix, Amazon Prime, HBO Go, and SlingTV, among many others. It also comes with plenty of free content via Roku’s own channel.

    4. Brass Pen

    In 57 Gadgets fourth one is This rollerball pen by Grovemade may be an analog technology, but it’s no less useful. Machined from solid brass, the writing instrument will help you take a break from the screen in the coolest possible fashion.

    5. S1 Stealth Phone Pocket

    The S1 Stealth Pocket by Dango Products is another one from 57 Gadgets, will instantly transform most phone cases into wallet replacements. It is designed to securely attach to the back of your current cover via a heavy-duty adhesive that’s sturdy yet easy to remove if you wish. You can even attach the S1 Stealth pocket to your phone without a case.

    6. eats Flex Wireless Earphones

    The Beats Flex are one of the best-performing wireless neckband earbuds in their price range, this is another one from 57 Gadgets you should definitely try this year.

    7. Walkman NW-A105 MP3 Player

    The Sony Walkman NW-A105 MP3 player has excellent audio quality, top-notch battery life (up to 26 hours between charges), expandable storage, and handy buttons for controlling the audio playback.     8. Smart Plug

    The WeMo mini smart plug by Belkin can work with Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, and the Apple HomeKit. It has a compact design and an effortless setup.

    9. USB-C Magnetic Charging Dock for Apple Watch

    This charging dock by Satechi will allow you to charge an Apple Watch by simply plugging it into a USB-C connector. You can use the accessory with most laptops, countless battery packs, and even a compatible car charger.

    10. CTRL Mechanical Keyboard

    The Drop CTRL mechanical keyboard has a compact design with an 87-key layout that guarantees a distraction-free typing experience. It has a sturdy machined aluminum frame, and you can order it with a wide selection of mechanical switches.

    11. Lightning Wired Earbuds

    Lightning Wired Earbuds are excellent in Gadgets. These reasonably priced Lightning earbuds are an excellent iPhone accessory. They have an understated design, a comfy fit, impressive audio quality for their price point, and a built-in remote control for taking calls.

     12. Basilisk Ultimate HyperSpeed Wireless Gaming Mouse

    The Razer Basilisk Ultimate is as excellent as a gaming mouse can be. Thanks to a cutting-edge optical sensor with 20,000 DPI, the accessory can deliver uncompromising, pro-grade performance in both wireless and wired mode.

    13. T7 Touch Portable SSD

    Gadgets like T7 Touch Portable SSD, sets itself apart from the competition with its built-in fingerprint scanner. The feature allows you to access your encrypted files with one touch. It’s compatible with Mac, PC, and mobile devices. It’s easy to set up via a desktop software suite or a mobile app.

    14. Vista 2 True Wireless Earbuds

    Jaybird’s Vista 2 true wireless earbuds are deservedly our favorite for Gadgets. They have a cool design, a comfy fit, a powerful sound that’s infinitely customizable via an app, and robust noise cancellation.

    15. Portable Home Speaker

    The Bose Portable Home Speaker has great sound and impressive versatility. Equipped with Wi-Fi, the device is compatible with Amazon Alexa and Google Assistant voice commands, as well as Apple AirPlay 2 wireless playback. It’s water-resistant, too.

    16. Instax Mini Link Smartphone Printer

    This compact photo printer by Fujifilm will create 1.8-by-2.4-inch hard copies of your smartphone photos with impressive speed. Available in white, denim, or pink, the Instax Mini Link can churn out images in just 12 seconds using the brand’s own instant film.

    17. Wi-Fi Smart Lock

    The latest August Wi-Fi Smart Lock is compatible with most deadbolts, so it can easily integrate just about any front door into a smart-home setup. It even allows you to keep your existing home keys.

    18. Life Q30 Wireless Headphones

    The Soundcore Life Q30 wireless noise-canceling headphones by Anker are one of the best under $100. The over-ear cans have an elegant design, a comfy fit, impressive sound quality, sophisticated noise cancellation, and up to 30 hours of battery life.

    19. SoundForm Elite Hi-Fi Smart Speaker + Wireless Charger

    The Belkin SoundForm Elite Hi-Fi smart speaker will entertain you with fantastic sound and wirelessly charge your smartphone

    20. My Passport Portable SSD

    The WD My Passport portable SSD has a sleek yet rugged metal housing built to survive drops from more than 6 feet. The gadget has a USB 3.2 Type-C connector for blisteringly fast data-transfer speeds. Available in blue, gray, gold, and red with up to 2TB of storage, it offers powerful encryption with password protection.

    21. CHAMP Pro Portable Battery Pack

    The CHAMP Pro portable battery pack by Nimble is sleek, dependable, and, best of all, eco-friendly. Its housing is made with recycled plastic, and it has recycled-paper packaging that’s biodegradable.

    22. Chromecast With Google TV Streaming Player

    The incredibly affordable gadgets is Chromecast with Google TV can stream 4K HDR content from a plethora of popular services, as well as from a smartphone, a tablet, or a laptop. Powered by the Google Assistant, the product allows users to easily and effortlessly search for content and control smart home products with voice commands.

    23. Xtreme 3 Waterproof Bluetooth Speaker

    Another one from 57 Gadgets is JBL’s Xtreme 3 waterproof Bluetooth speaker will bring party-level sound anywhere you take it. It can blast powerful sound with excellent quality, thanks to a pair of woofers, a duo of tweeters, and passive radiators.

    24. Airmega 150 HEPA Air Purifier

    Another one from 57 Gadgets is ,The reasonably priced Coway Airmega 150 is our favorite HEPA air purifier for small spaces (up to 214 square feet). It has a minimalist design and a reliable air filtration setup. The last-mentioned bit includes a HEPA and a deodorization filter, as well as a washable pre-filter for larger airborne particles.

    25. SmartSleep Wake-up Light

    Another one from 57 Gadgets is SmartSleep Wake-up Light from Philips is designed to improve your mood in the morning with a more natural alarm. It comes with 5 different sunrise and sunset settings, as well as 10 different brightness settings if you want to use your smart alarm clock as a reading light, too.

    26. HomePod mini Smart Speaker

    Another one from 57 Gadgets is The palm-sized Apple HomePod mini smart speaker is also a convenient HomeKit hub. The sub-$100 gadget allows users to control a number of smart home products with Siri voice commands or directly from an Apple device. It’s available in white or space gray.

    27. QuietComfort 45 Wireless Headphones

    Another one from 57 Gadgets is The Bose QuietComfort 45 wireless headphones, as their model number suggests, are a follow up to the iconic QuietComfort 35 noise-canceling cans. The new iteration delivers next-level performance, futureproof wireless connectivity, and a USB-C connector for charging.

    28. Everyday Zip Backpack

    The latest iteration of the Peak Design Everyday backpack is the best yet. A must-have for on-the-go gearheads, the accessory has an elegant design, a fully customizable interior, and a wraparound zip enclosure.

    29. WLPP1CFH-1 Smart Plug

    This incredibly affordable smart plug by Wyze has a cool design, intuitive app controls, and compatibility with Amazon Alexa and Google Assistant voice commands. It will allow you to set custom schedules for connected appliances and, thanks to a vacation mode, randomly power lights on and off when you are away from home.

    30. Galaxy Watch 4 Smartwatch

    The Samsung Galaxy Watch4 smartwatch is the best from 57 gadgets. Powered by Google’s WearOS, it has a robust set of health-, fitness-, and sleep-tracking features, including the ability to automatically track your workouts. The timepiece is waterproof up to 50 meters, so it’s perfect for swimmers, too.

    31. Video Doorbell

    Another one from 57 Gadgets is The Google Nest Doorbell will allow you to see and talk to visitors, even while away from home. The device can stream high-resolution video with a wide field of view, including in the dark. Thanks to onboard machine learning, it can alert you if it notices a person, a package delivery, an animal, or a vehicle.

    32. HERO10 Black Waterproof Action Camera

    Another one from 57 Gadgets is The HERO10 Black by GoPro is, unsurprisingly, an even better variant of the best action camera out there. It’s compact, easy to operate (we like its front-facing LCD display), and full of connectivity features. It can capture super sharp 5.3K video, 23MP still images, as well as livestream your adventures in high resolution.

    33. Pro UV Sanitizer & Charger

    Another one from 57 Gadgets is The PhoneSoap Pro UV sanitizer is a favorite of ours because it can rapidly and efficiently rid your essential items from potentially dangerous germs. According to its manufacturer, the device’s onboard UV-C light is even effective against COVID-19.

    34. MX Keys Mini Wireless Keyboard

    Another one from 57 Gadgets is Logitech’s MX Keys Mini is the compact and versatile Bluetooth keyboard that many have been looking for. It has a compact footprint, perfectly spaced backlit keys with exceptional tactility, and, best of all, an emoji shortcut button. The accessory can pair with and seamlessly switch between any desktop, laptop, smartphone, or tablet.

    35. Amazon Echo Dot Smart Speaker

    The latest Amazon Echo Dot smart speaker has an orb-like design that’s cooler than its predecessor’s. The Alexa-powered gadget also has a stylish and understated fabric cover.

    36. Quatro Wireless Power Bank

    The sleek Satechi Quatro battery pack can charge an Apple Watch, as well as a trio of additional gadgets on the go. In addition to an Apple Watch dock, it packs a wireless charging pad and a pair of USB ports (USB-C and USB-A).

    37. Fit Pro True Wireless Earbuds

    The Beats Fit Pro earphones are a workout-ready version of the brand’s Studio Buds. They offer a more secure fit via integrated wingtips to go with signature Beats sound and excellent noise cancellation.

    38. Samsung Galaxy Tab A8 Android Tablet

    The Samsung Galaxy Tab A8 is a full-size Android tablet with an elegant design, stellar hardware specs, and impressive performance. Best of all, the slate has a reasonable price that starts at just a tad above $200.

    39. TUF Gaming VG32VQR Curved Monitor (32-inch)

    Another one from 57 Gadgets is The ASUS TUF Gaming VG32VQR curved gaming monitor has it all: a cool design with slim bezels, a large display with fantastic picture quality, and superb gaming performance. The latter comes courtesy of onboard AMD FreeSync and NVIDIA G-SYNC tech.

    40. Google Pixel 6 Android Phone

    The Pixel 6 by Google is our favorite Android phone for most consumers. It has a sleek and durable design and top-shelf hardware. Its highlights include a powerful chip, a vibrant display, and a great camera setup. Best of all, the latest Pixel has an incredibly reasonable price that starts below $600.

    41. Signature M650 Wireless Mouse

    The latest Signature M650 by Logitech is one of the best wireless options out there. The mouse has an ultra-precise scrolling wheel, super silent buttons, and a comfy grip, all for less than $40.

    42. Pro Bluetooth Tracker

    The updated Tile Pro tracker will help you find anything you attach it to, so long as your phone is within its Bluetooth range of 400 feet. In case you lose an item, you’ll receive notifications when the gadget comes within range of another Tile user.

    43. Everett Hybrid Smartwatch

    The Fossil Everett is a feature-packed hybrid smartwatch with a built-in, always-on display and heart rate sensor. We like that — instead of looking like a tech product — it resembles a classic chronograph timepiece with mechanical hands and a three-button layout.

    44. Orbi Pro WiFi 6 Mini AX 1800 Router

    Another one from 57 Gadgets is The Orbi Pro mesh Wi-Fi system by Netgear is reasonably priced, incredibly capable, and plenty futureproof. A single router can cover up to 2,000 square feet of space with up-to-date Wi-Fi 6 connectivity. You can expand the coverage by adding more satellites.

    45. SoundLink Waterproof Bluetooth Speaker

    Another one from 57 Gadgets is The all-new Bose SoundLink Flex waterproof Bluetooth speaker is a solid contender for the product category’s best offering. Available in black, white, and blue, it has an elegant design and an IP67 waterproof housing that can even float in water.

    46. Torch USB Microphone

    The sub-$100 Roccat Torch USB microphone is easy to set up and use, and it’s capable of streaming and recording studio-grade sound. It has mixer-style controls and incredibly cool RGB lighting.

    47. E5 Robot Vacuum and Mop

    The reasonably priced Roborock E5 is a robot vacuum with excellent cleaning capabilities, a quiet operation, and clever room-mapping tech. Unlike many rivals in its price range, the robot can even mop floors.

    48. iXpand Flash Drive Luxe

    The swiveling SanDisk iXpand Flash Drive Luxe is tailor-made for Apple hardware users. It’s rocking a USB-C and a Lightning connector, which make transferring images, video, and other files between Apple products and other USB-C-equipped devices insanely effortless.

    49. M1 Maverick Bifold Wallet

    The M1 Maverick bifold wallet by Dango Products is an everyday accessory that effortlessly combines cool design, exceptional craftsmanship, and a host of useful features. Dango offers the badass-looking accessory in a multitude of leather and metal options.

    50. Apple Watch Series 7

    The thoroughly redesigned Apple Watch Series 7 has a bigger case and a larger screen than its predecessor. Of course, the product also has best-in-class health-, fitness-, and wellness-tracking capabilities, powered by accurate heart rate and blood oxygen sensors.

    51. Bites 2 Pet Camera with Treat Dispenser

    This is a cool tech gadgets for all the pet owners out there. The sleek Petcube Bites 2 camera allows you to keep an eye on your pet while you’re not home through Wi-Fi connectivity, and can even deploy a treat for them right from your smartphone.

    52. Essentials A19 Smart Lightbulb

    The Nanoleaf Essentials A19 smart lightbulb is a must-have for Apple HomeKit users and worth every penny of its impulse-buy price tag. The connected home gadget takes seconds to set up, because it doesn’t require a hub — you need an iPad, a HomePod mini, or an Apple TV instead.

    53. Link & Lock Carabiner for Apple AirTags

    The Raptic Link & Lock carabiner is one of the coolest accessories for Apple AirTags we’ve encountered yet. Crafted from ultra-tough zinc, the product has a heavy-duty lock with a three-digit combination code. The standout feature makes tampering with your favorite tracker and removing it from an item practically impossible.

    54. AirTag Tracker

    Another one from 57 Gadgets is Apple’s affordable AirTag tracker is a must-have tech gadget for any iPhone owner, because it can seamlessly keep tabs on just about anything you attach it to. When out of your iPhone’s range, the AirTag will update its location when it comes in proximity to a device that’s part of Apple’s Find My network (there are almost a billion of them).

    55. Connected Cube

    This is a reimagined version of one of the most iconic toys ever made. With onboard Bluetooth connectivity and a companion app, the Rubik’s Connected cube will teach you how to solve the notoriously challenging, incredibly interactive puzzle and have fun in the process. Rather impressively, the Rubik’s Connected app will also allow users to keep track of their progress and access stats to help improve their solving game. There’s also an option to join a community of users and compete with them.

    56. Shiatsu Back and Neck Massager

    There’s nothing better than a good massage — except for a good massage that never has to end. Another one from 57 Gadgets is This shiatsu massager can be used at home or even in the car (!) and is great for the shoulders, back, calves, and more.

    57. Metro Wind-Resistant Umbrella

    Thanks to a whole lot of innovative engineering, Another one from 57 Gadgets is the Blunt Metro umbrella was built to withstand wind gusts up to 72 mph. Available in a multitude of colors, the product has patented canopy tips, which are not only part of its tensioning system but also safe for the people around you. The umbrella is also comprised of fiberglass ribs and a canopy crafted from high-density, water-repellent fabric.
