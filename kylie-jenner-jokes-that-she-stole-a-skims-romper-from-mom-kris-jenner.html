    Kylie Jenner's latest Instagram photo dump has caught the attention of her big sister Kim Kardashian.

    SKIMS RAW EDGE ONESIE

    SKIMS RAW EDGE ONESIE

    The Kylie Cosmetics founder recently shared photos of herself on Instagram, in which she poses on a grassy slope. Jenner looks effortlessly cool in a gray-mauve body-clinging romper, which features a backless silhouette, and black knee-high boots. She accessorizes sparingly in the pastoral setting, wearing only a pair of black shades, and slicks her hair up into her signature gelled-back high bun.

    The romper, as it turns out, hails from Kardashian's shapewear line, SKIMS. The mom of four commented on her little sister's post, "can u tag @skims please LOL."

    As of today (Tuesday, January 17), Jenner has yet to update her post or caption with a tag crediting her sister's brand. Though, the makeup mogul did comment back, "@kimkardashian i had to steal this from moms house and now you want me to promote ?!!!! wow @skims."

    Lately, Jenner has taken to Instagram to document her fashion escapades. While vacationing with sister Kendall Jenner and friend Hailey Bieber in Aspen earlier this month, she showed out in multiple snow bunny looks.

    In one post, she wore a retro flared coat by Alaïa as a minidress, which she styled with a beige turtleneck, sheer tights, and knee-high black leather boots with white stitching. Her accessories included black shades and gloves, a furry black bucket hat, and a structural pyramid bag.

    In another post, Jenner put a sultry spin on après-ski style, wearing a white bathrobe with nothing underneath except a black vinyl bikini from Good American.
