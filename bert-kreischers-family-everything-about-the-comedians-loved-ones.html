    Bert Kreischer’s family life has been the subject of attention from fans for years. Here’s everything we know about this close-knit family. 

    Who Is Bert Kreischer?

    Bert Kreischer, born “Albert Charles Kreischer Jr,” is an American stand-up comedian, podcaster, reality television star, and actor. He rose to fame when he was featured in an article in Rolling Stone as a student at Florida State University in 1997. 

    The magazine named Bert “the top partner at the Number One Party School in the country.” The article went viral as expected and launched Bert into stardom. The report also inspired the 2002 film “National Lampoon’s Van Wilder.”

    Since then, Bert has built his career into an established one in the entertainment industry. He also acts, does stand-up shows, hosts reality TV shows, and features in podcasts. 

    Bert Kreischer’s Family 

    Bert is known as a dedicated family man, and from his jokes and interviews, one can tell that he enjoys his role a lot. This has made people curious about the comedian’s family and their dynamics. Keep reading to learn about Bert Kreischer’s family and their personal lives. 

    Bert Kreischer’s Spouse

    The reality TV host is married to a beautiful woman named Leann Kreischer. She was born on August 19, 1971, in Bowdon, Georgia. She spent most of her life in Bowdon, where she had her earlier education and attended high school. 

    After graduating high school, Leeann furthered her education by enrolling at the University of Georgia to study an undisclosed course. After graduating from the university, she moved to Los Angeles, California, to break into the Hollywood industry. 

    Leeann started as a writer and is credited as a writer for the teen comedy “My Biggest Fan.” She also tried her hand at acting but did not pursue it passionately. She played the role of a nurse in the short film “Destiny Stalled,” and was also featured in “Well Done with Sebastian Maniscalco” and “The Cabin with Kreischer” in 2000, 2020, and 2021, respectively. 

    Leeann also has a podcast, just like her husband. Her weekly show is titled “Wife of the Party” and features several guests, like her friends, talking about their lives, loved ones, and adventures. The celebrity wife’s podcast has about 300 episodes and is rated 4.9/5 by thousands. 

    She met her husband Bert when his flatmate brought him along to her office on a visit. They met the second time at a yoga class but didn’t intend to date until they met for a third time at a bowling party Bert’s partner organized. 

    The third time was the charm. The couple started dating and have been together ever since. 

    How Old Are Bert Kreischer’s Daughters?

    Bret and Leeann have two beautiful daughters, Georgia and Ila, from their union. They are 19 and 16 years old as of 2023. Their first child, Georgia, is a celebrity kid who has captured the public’s attention due to her famous parents and vibrant personality. She was born on June 8, 2004, and resides with her parents and sister in Los Angeles, California. 

    Georgia’s younger sister, Ila, was born on July 19, 2007. She is nicknamed “Ilaker” by her family and loved ones and can be seen on her parent’s socials. She also appeared alongside her father in the TV series “The Cabin with Bert Kreischer” in 2020. Ila attended a private school in Los Angeles, where she grew up with her family.

    Both daughters are still teenagers, and most of their lives are private. 

    Bert constantly talks about his wife and two kids on his podcast or during stand-up shows. He talks about how they are his top priority, and his heart longs for them whenever he’s away from them for a while. 

    He also frequently talks about his experiences as a father and his trials and errors as a parent to two daughters. The family appears to be loving, and the world shows them a lot of love whenever they step out as a unit or individually. 

    Bert Kreischer’s Family on Family Feud

    Bert’s family was featured on the fourth episode of the ninth season of the famous Steve Harvey TV show “Family Feud.” The episode aired on July 30, 2023, with 3.62 million viewers. 

    The episode was titled “WWE Women vs. WWE Men and Marcus Lemonis vs. Bert Kreischer.” Bert’s family and Marcus Lemonis went head to head on the show for the prize. Both families were excited and shared backstage photographs of themselves before the show.
