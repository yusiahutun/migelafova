    Nicole Kidman's Abs Became an Accessory in This Cutout-Covered Dress

    Nicole Kidmans date night style (red carpet edition) just got a whole lot edgier. The Big Little Lies star made a jaw-dropping appearance at this years Country Music Association Awards in Nashville with husband Keith Urban.

    05 Mar 2024, 00:00

    Nicole Kidman’s date night style (red carpet edition) just got a whole lot edgier.

    The Big Little Lies star made a jaw-dropping appearance at this year’s Country Music Association Awards in Nashville with husband Keith Urban.

    For the star-studded event, Kidman showed out in a sleek, modern black dress by Coperni, featuring a plunging neckline and a huge ab-baring cutout across the middle. A silver triangular ring connected the bottom of her bustier to the rest of the gown.

    Kidman finished off the look with more black elements, including black Jimmy Choo pumps and a black leather handbag. She also embraced a fashion faux pas by mixing metals in her Rainbow K jewelry, which included stacks of silver and gold rings, a silver chrome bracelet, and a diamond-encrusted gold wraparound necklace.

    Getty Images

    As for Urban, the singer looked casual in a dark gray button-up, black trousers, and chunky black boots. His accessories included a geometric bracelet and an array of rings.

    The pair looked so in love while cozying up together at the glamorous event.

    Getty Images

    John Shearer//Getty Images

    This isn’t the first time Kidman has delivered a major fashion statement in a black dress with cutouts.

    During a special screening of her Paramount+ series Special Ops: Lioness in July, the actor opted for an all-black Saint Laurent look. This included an inky-black gown with a crossover halter neckline with long gauzy panels, a large rosette adorning the bust, and a diamond-shaped abdominal cutout that revealed her torso and underboob. To accessorize, she added black sheer opera gloves and black pointed-toe strappy heels.
