    Hottest and Coldest Temperatures Ever in Pittsburgh on November 25

    01 Mar 2024, 00:00

    On November 25th, Pittsburgh experienced a range of temperatures, with the highest ever recorded being 66.9°F in 1987 and the lowest being 7.0°F in 1950. The average temperature for this date is 37.62°F, with an average high of 45.30°F and an average low of 29.94°F. In 1987, when the hottest temperature was recorded, the iconic movie "Dirty Dancing" was released, becoming a worldwide hit and popularizing the memorable line, "Nobody puts Baby in a corner."
