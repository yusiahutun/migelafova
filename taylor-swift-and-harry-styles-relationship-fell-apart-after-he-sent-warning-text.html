    With Taylor Swift releasing her 1989 Taylor’s Version, fans are looking back on a slew of heartthrobs Taylor dated in the past. One in particular has caught fans’ eyes, it took place during the original recording of the album. A high profile British musician who seemingly inspired her song Style, has been talk of the town. Yes, we are talking about Harry Styles.

    The epic romance between Taylor Swift and Harry Styles was fairly short-lived. But that has never stopped the music legend from creating masterpieces from her heartbreaks. While the details of their relationship were never made public, music fans are joining the dots from newly released vault tracks of 1989. Paired with insider knowledge hinting at the reasons Taylor called it quits on her London Boy, we’ve made a discovery.

    Taylor Swift and Harry Styles’ relationship was talk of Tinsel Town

    Taylor Swift and Harry Styles started dating at the end of 2012. A source close to Taylor reportedly claimed the British celebrity ‘wore her down’ after pursuing her for a while. The source said Styles apparently showered her with compliments.

    Recalling the tumultuous time, the source revealed: “And then, last spring, she said, there was a weekend “where they got really close, and he was all, like, ‘You’re amazing—I want to be with you. I want to do this.’”

    Soon after the beginning of Harry and Taylor’s ‘love story,’ Styles apparently went on tour in Australia with his band One Direction.

    However, this is when heartbreak struck.

    He allegedly sent a text to Swift warning her of a picture of himself on the Internet where he is “kissing a friend goodbye.” Harry apparently claimed: “It’s no big deal.”

    When Taylor looked for the picture online, she found that they were “making out like with their hands all up in each other’s hair”, which ‘made’ her call it quits – the source says.

    “She feels like he’s looking at every girl”

    Taylor and Harry apparently went back to each other ‘a few times’ and fans speculate Taylor’s We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together could be about Harry as well. As per a source, Taylor felt like Harry was “looking at every girl and thinking about hooking up with every girl.”

    Taylor’s 1989 album consists of multiple songs which portray this anxiety in relationships, including Out Of The Woods. While the 12-time Grammy-winning celebrity has never officially confirmed which songs are about whom, fans are now guessing that Taylor’s latest 1989 TV vault track Is It Over Now? could be about Styles as it matches quite a few details.

    Joking about how fans wanted a Harry Styles feature in the new album, one fan posted on Elon Musk’s X (formerly Twitter) that it finally arrived.

    They wrote: “Y’all wanted a Harry Styles collab and we kinda got it… Is It Over Now? (feat. Harry Styles shredded to pieces) (Taylor’s version) (from the vault).”

    While Taylor has not openly talked about the celebrity couple‘s official status, a representative for Styles, Benny Taranti at Columbia Records, called all of Swift’s source’s claims “undeniably false.”
