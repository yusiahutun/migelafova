    19 Mar 2024, 00:00

    Sydney Sweeney looked like a true Hollywood bombshell last night at the Variety Power of Young Hollywood event in Los Angeles.

    The Euphoria star made an entrance in a striking all-black ensemble and timeless glam. She wore a skintight black leather Alexander McQueen co-ord consisting of a sculpted, corseted top with a peplum detail, plus a long biker skirt with a high leg slit. She finished the look with black heels with silver points and silver jewelry, including various rings and large abstract hoop earrings.

    Frazer Harrison//Getty Images

    Sweeney’s glam was an elegant take on the rocker look. The actor wore a pointy electric-blue manicure, subtle silver eye shadow, rosy blush, a glossy pink lip, and a soft, sophisticated, wavy hairdo.

    Monica Schipper//Getty Images

    For the evening event, Sweeney brought along her cousin Kaden Ryan. He walked the carpet with the star and posed for photos with her while looking dapper in a black suit, crisp white dress shirt, and white sneakers.

    Monica Schipper//Getty Images

    Sweeney’s red-carpet style never misses. At this year’s Met Gala, the actor was a vision in a ruched blush gown by Miu Miu embellished with clear jewels and large silver sequins all over. The curve-hugging soft pink dress additionally featured a breezy skirt and a large black bow at the bottom. Sweeney finished the maximalist look with a matching black bow in her hair, diamond rings and earrings by Bucherer Fine Jewellery, and even a jewel-encrusted pointy manicure.

    And later in May, while attending the Cannes Film Festival, Sweeney stepped out looking lovely in a silky white slipdress by Miu Miu, which she layered over an also silky powder-blue bra. She left the lingerie exposed and finished the look with white platform heels with silver chain straps, and small diamond hoop earrings.
