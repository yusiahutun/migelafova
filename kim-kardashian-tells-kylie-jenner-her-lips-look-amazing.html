      Kylie Jenner caused a stir last week when, after months of speculation, she admitted to having temporary lip fillers injected into her pout. Beauty icon Kim Kardashian revealed in an episode of that she completely supports her half-sister's decision.

      Kylie Jenner spoke to Kim Kardashian and Kendall Jenner about her lips

      "Your lips look amazing," said the 34-year-old. "I don't want you to get carried away, lips aren't permanent, but if [the media] ask about your lips, own up to it."
