    How to pay for GOtv packages using Mpesa?

    Below is step by step guide you can follow to pay for your package using the Mpesa option.

  • Go to your Mpesa menu.
  • Select "Lipa Na Mpesa".
  • Select "PayBill".
  • Select "Business Number".
  • Enter 423655.
  • Enter your Gotv IUC number (at the bottom of your decoder with a red sticker).
  • Enter the amount of money you want to pay.
  • Enter your Mpesa pin.
  • Press "OK".
  • Wait for the Mpesa confirmation message.
