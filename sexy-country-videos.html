    Sometimes the sexiest country videos are the ones where the artists get to, well, be themselves. Whether it's Shania Twain's self declaration or Tim McGraw caught on camera with his stunning wife Faith Hill, these videos are steaming hot. Of course, there's some eye candy, too -- from models to the the good-looking leading men and women of country, such as Keith Urban, Miranda Lambert and Dierks Bentley. Check out what makes our favorite artists "melt" in our list of sexy country videos!

    10. Trace Adkins, 'Hot Mama'
    In the eyes of Trace Adkins, we start this list tipping our hats off to all of the moms that have still got it. Adkins can't stop daydreaming of his "wife" in lingerie, as well as a bikini, while she attempts to do her soccer mom chores. You can't really blame him for fantasizing -- it's his wife, after all.

    9. Miranda Lambert, 'Kerosene'
    Attitude, ripped jeans, arson antics and bada--- guitar playing set the tone for this sexy country video. After Miranda Lambert finds her boyfriend in bed with another woman, she marks his trail with kerosene, burning the path and ultimately his house. Never mind the never-ending kerosene tank -- she gets the job done!

    8. Toby Keith, 'Bullet in the Gun'
    Winner of the ACA's 2010 Video Visionary Award, Toby Keith tells the story of a drifter who meets his soulmate -- who happens to be an exotic dancer. After the two find themselves in trouble with the law, they embark on a 'Thelma and Louise' Western outlaw adventure that includes shoot-outs, stealing, motorcycle riding and some action at a Mexican motel. H-O-T!

    7. Brooks and Dunn, 'Ain't Nothing 'Bout You'
    Brooks and Dunn's longest-lasting No. 1 song to date gets star treatment in the Trey Fanjoy directed video. Actress Alana De La Garza poses as a model in the video, donning fur hats and loose grandma sweaters over lingerie for her photo shoot. "There ain't nothing 'bout you that don't do something for me," the boys sing about their lady in red.

    6. Rascal Flatts, 'I Melt'
    "A lot of women would like to hear a man say they donâ€™t have to do anything special to make him melt," Gary LeVox of Rascal Flatts says about the melodic song. Aside from the romantic gesture, the video integrates sex appeal, showing a fair amount of nudity, including (gasp!) Joe Don Rooney's bare behind.

    5. Faith Hill, 'Breathe'
    The title track off Faith Hill's Grammy Award-winning album may have been her seventh No. 1 single, but the music video revealed, for the first time, a more more sultry side of her. Much like Shania Twain's video for 'That Don't Impress Me Much,' Faith is shown in sexy fare in desert scenery. Although she wears only a sheet in the bed scenes, she stays a class act.

    4. Dierks Bentley, 'Come a Little Closer'
    It's sexy enough that Bentley can master bluegrass. But before he released 'Up on the Ridge,' he won us over with his sultry voice in this No.1 hit from 2005. Who can say "no" to a guy serenading us with his guitar in bed? "Come a little closer, baby / I feel like layin' you down," he croons.

    3. Keith Urban, 'Someone Like You'
    Keith Urban rocks out on banjo and electric guitar in this desert-centric video, proving he's just as good in concert as behind the camera. Before Nicole Kidman, there was ex-girlfriend Niki Taylor, who makes a cameo appearance.

    2. Faith Hill and Tim McGraw, 'Let's Make Love'
    What better way to capture love than a black-and -white music video set in Paris? It doesn't hurt when the leads are none other than country's sexiest power couple. Hill and McGraw have undeniable chemistry both vocally and on camera.

    1. Shania Twain, 'Man! I Feel Like a Woman'
    Shania Twain plays the "leading man" in this spot, a play off of Robert Palmer's 'Addicted to Love.' Performing in a form fitting tailored coat and top hat, she leaves the eye candy role to her amateur guitar-playing sidekicks in pleather pants.
