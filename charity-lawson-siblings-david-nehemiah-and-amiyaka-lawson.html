    Meet the three siblings of Charity Lawson who has been announced as the leading lady for Bachelorette season 20. All of them are older than her, they are named David Lawson Jr., Nehemiah Lawson, and Amiyaka Lawson. How old are they? What do they do for a living?

    Read all about them as you scroll down.

    Who Are Bachelorette Star Charity Lawson Siblings?

    New Bachelorette for season 20 has been announced and her name is Charity Lawson, from season 27 of Bachelor aka the season of Zach Shallcross. She was eliminated right after the hometown episode. While fans were displeased by her exit from the show, the recent announcement is something to be excited about.

    Though Charity’s career is like an open book, she is very private about her family members. However, we’ve done some digging up and discovered a few details about her family members.

    The leading lady of Bachelorette season 20 was born as the youngest of four children of David E Lawson and Vickie C Lawson of Columbus, Georgia. Both of her parents are 64 years old as they were born in 1958. Furthermore, David is working in Aflac whereas his wife appears to be a dentist.

    However, it’s time to meet her three siblings. From here on, you’ll discover details related to their age, job, and social media reach.

    Meet David Lawson Jr., Charity Lawson Brother

    David Lawson Jr. is the oldest of three siblings of Charity Lawson. Back in January 2023, David announced his sister’s appearance on Bachelor with a FB post. He captioned it, “Y’all tune in The Bachelor this upcoming season premiere Jan 23rd at 8 pm, my lil sister Charity will be on the show.”

    When Charity completed her board exam, David proudly announced, “Baby sister passed Boards, a nationally certified counselor Refer to me now as Charity Lawson, M.Ed, NCC…#Lawsonfamilywealth #GrindModeOn.”

    On Charity’s 26th birthday in December 2021, David wished his sister, “Happy 26th bday to my youngest sister Charity, the baby girl and the silly one like me. Love you baby girl. Enjoy your vacation.”

    • David Lawson Jr. Age

    David Lawson Jr. is currently 41 years old. He was born in October 1981.

    • David Lawson Jr. Job

    David Lawson Jr. seems to have a job in the catering business. However, not many details surfaced about his job.

    • Is David Lawson Jr. On Instagram?

    No, David Lawson Jr. is not on Instagram. However, it has Facebook.

    Meet Nehemiah Lawson, Charity Lawson Brother

    Nehemiah Lawson appeared during her hometown episode in Bachelor. He shared his thoughts on Zach admiring him saying that Zach “seems like a standup guy”. He added, “as a brother, I’m overprotective of my sister because she deserves the best.” Nehemiah said in the episode that he doesn’t want to see his sister get hurt after her bad breakup in the past.

    Charity also explained that her brother is “my best friend” and “my rock”.

    • Nehemiah Lawson Age

    Nemehmiah Lawson was born in October 1993. In 2023, she is currently 29 years old.

    • Nehemiah Lawson Job

    Nehemiah Lawson started his career as a Former Model/Impact at Hollister Co. He was a former Microbiology Lab Assistant at the University of Georgia from 2011 to 2014. In 2013, Nehemiah interned at West Georgia Oral Surgery Associates, P.C.

    Other career details that his resume boasts are, Nehemiah is a President at UGA Accidentals, Event services at Georgia Center – the University of Georgia Conference Center and Hotel, a Student at UNC Adams School of Dentistry, and a pediatric Resident Dentist at Children’s Medical Center Dallas.

    From 2019 to 2021, Nehemiah worked at Texas A&M University Baylor College of Dentistry as a Pediatric Resident Dentist. Starting in 2021, he is working as Pediatric Dentist at Southpark Pediatric Dentistry.

    • Is Nehemiah Lawson On Instagram?

    Yes, Nehemiah Lawson is on Facebook (@nehemiah.lawson) and Instagram (@_thesingingdentist).

    Meet Amiyaka Lawson, Charity Lawson Sister

    Amiyaka Lawson is the second-born child and older sister of Charity Lawson. After the official announcement, Charity announced, “Say hello to your new Bachelorette!,” while posting the announcement from the official page of The Bachelor.

    • Amiyaka Lawson Age

    As of March 2023, Amiyaka Lawson is 43 years old. 1979 is her birth year.

    • Amiyaka Lawson Job

    Amiyaka Lawson, per her LinkedIn, is working as Group Lead at TSYS. She attended Austin High School, Pemberton Township High School, and Baumholder American High School, in Germany. She completed her college education at Columbus State University.

    • Is Amiyaka Lawson On Instagram?

    No, Amiayaka Lawson is not on Instagram. But you can find her on Facebook.

    Related FAQs

    • Where Do Charity Lawson Siblings Reside?

    Charity Lawson’s two siblings David Jr. and Amiayaka are currently residing in Columbus, Georgia. Her brother Nehemiah is residing in Charlotte, North Carolina.

    • Have Charity Lawson Siblings Appeared On TV?

    Only Charity Lawson’s brother Nehemiah appeared on The Bachelor’s hometown episode. Fans admired him to be a loving brother to Charity.
