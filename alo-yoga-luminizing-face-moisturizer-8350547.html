    For a majority of us, our skin changes with the seasons. Regardless of having typically dry, oily, or combination skin, we all could use help with hydration in the fall and winter. The formulas you’ve been using all summer long just aren’t going to cut it when the wind picks up and the temperatures drop. 

    One easy change you can make to your regimen is by swapping out your lightweight moisturizer for one that’s deeply hydrating. Alo Yoga, known for its activewear worn by celebrities like Katie Holmes and Jennifer Garner, also has a line of skincare products that includes the Alo Yoga Luminizing Face Moisturizer, which shoppers can’t stop raving about for its nourishing benefits and dewy finish.

    Alo Yoga Luminizing Face Moisturizer

    The $48 formula combines soothing aloe, nourishing avocado oil (that also provides skin with vitamin E for firmness), moisture-rich squalane, and sweet almond with vitamins A and D to improve discoloration. Its hero ingredient is amla — also known as a super berry — which is rich in antioxidants and vitamin C and has been used in Indian Ayurvedic healing for thousands of years, according to the brand. Its anti-aging benefits promote cell turnover while brightening skin for a more even, plump, and youthful complexion. 

    The brand recommends incorporating the moisturizer into both your morning and nighttime routines after cleansing. It’s suitable for all skin types in addition to being hypoallergenic, noncomedogenic, and unscented. If you tend to have oilier skin but need a hydration boost every now and again, one shopper recommends using it just at night since it is on the thicker side. 

    A reviewer who often deals with acne breakouts said the formula “doesn’t clog [their] pores,” while another person called it simply “heavenly.” A 40-year-old customer with sensitive skin said the formula “makes [them] feel beautiful again,” adding that it gives them back the “glow” they had in their 20s while also leaving their skin “bright and perky.” It also leaves skin “radiant,” according to a fourth shopper. 

    Grab your own jar of the Luminizing Face Moisturizer at Alo Yoga and keep scrolling for other shopper-loved skincare products.
