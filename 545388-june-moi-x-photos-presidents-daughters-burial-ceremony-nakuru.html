    June Moi: 5 Photos from Ex-President's Daughter's Burial Ceremony in Nakuru

    Former President Daniel Moi's daughter, June Chebet, has finally been laid to rest. TUKO.co.ke has featured five photos describing how her funeral went down. June Moi was buried in her home in Nakuru, on Thursday, April 18.

    03 Aug 2024, 00:00
    • The funeral marked the final goodbye to June Chebet Moi, the daughter of former President Daniel Moi, in a solemn ceremony held in Nakuru
    • Several prominent political figures, including Kalonzo Musyoka and David Maraga, attended the funeral to show solidarity with the family
    • June's daughter, Paula Jepkemboi, delivered a moving tribute, expressing her deep love and sorrow for her mother's passing

    Former President Daniel Moi's daughter, June Chebet, has finally been laid to rest.

    TUKO.co.ke has featured five photos describing how her funeral went down.

    1. Buried in Nakuru

    June Moi was buried in her home in Nakuru, on Thursday, April 18.

    2. Lavish funeral convoy

    The body arrived from the Kabarak Airstrip to the Kabarak University grounds in a lavish hearse, trailed by a heavy motorcade.

    3. Ranking politicians attend June Moi's funeral

    The funeral was attended by several politicians who are friends with Moi's family.

    Wiper Party leader Kalonzo Musyoka, former cabinet secretary of the Ministry of Defense Eugine Wamalwa and former chief justice David Maraga attended the funeral. showing solidarity with Gideon Moi.

    4. June Moi's daughter's emotional tribute

    June Moi's daughter Paula Jepkemboi broke down while remembering her memories with her mum.

    Jepkemboi showered her mum with love in her tribute.

    She said:

    "If Roses Grow in Heaven. If roses grow in heaven, Lord, please pick a bunch for me. Place them in my mother's arms and tell her they're from me. Tell her I love her and miss her, and when she turns to smile, place a kiss on her cheek and hold her for a while. -Because remembering her is easy. I do it every day, - but there's an ache within my heart that will never go away. Mama, I promise to be strong and make you proud.

    5. Colourful but sombre ceremony

    The funeral was perfectly decorated but a sombre mood was in the air.

    Details about June Moi

    June was announced dead on Thursday, April 11, aged 60 years.

    She succumbed to a bout of an undisclosed illness; she was the last-born daughter of the former president.

    She was adopted by the late retired president at a young age.

    June attended the Nairobi Primary School and later proceeded to Kenya High School for her A levels before moving to Canada to pursue her tertiary education.
