      Ryan Reynolds and Rosario Dawson may have been in Cannes to premiere their latest film but it was Rosario’s new haircut which had everyone talking. The gorgeous actress had shaved half of her hair off, sweeping the remaining hair to one side to show off the drastic new look.

      Wearing an abstract art print dress with a flattering peplum style, Rosario looked every inch the star on the Crosiette. Ryan and Rosario were kicking off promotional duties for new film , which also stars Mirelle Enos. Ryan and Mirelle play parents whose daughter was kidnapped eight years previously. Across a fragmented narrative, Rosario’s detective attempts to unravel the mystery of the daughter who they believe is still alive.

      Ryan arrived in Cannes on Thursday and is expected on tonight’s red carpet with wife Blake Lively who has been in the French Riviera since Tuesday.
